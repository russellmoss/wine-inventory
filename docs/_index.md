### How to use this vault (30-second version)
- **Wikilinks:** type `[[` and Obsidian autocompletes note names. Click any `[[link]]` to jump.
- **Backlinks:** bottom of every note shows what links *to* it — your trail of connections.
- **Graph view:** left ribbon → graph icon. It literally draws your architecture as you link things.
- **Search:** `Ctrl+Shift+F`. **Quick open:** `Ctrl+O`.
- You and Claude write to the *same files*. Anything you add here, Claude can read as context.
