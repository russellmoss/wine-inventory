# 6/13/2025 Product Update: Appellation Roll-up, Reporting Enhancements, Efficiency Boosts, and SUPPLY!

Product updates for MAKE through June 13, 2025 include [Appellation Roll-up](#Appellation), [report improvements](#reporting) for costing, bottling & vessel reports, [better usability](#efficent) for work order printing, Tank Maps & BOLs, and adding a [new lab source](#lab).

We've also launched our new Case Goods Management product, [SUPPLY](#supply)!  Stay tuned for additional SUPPLY features and releases to be included in our product updates!

### Features/Improvements

#### Appellation Roll-up

We've just made it easier for California wineries to aggregate lot composition across smaller but overlapping geographic groupings. If your lot components come from a variety of smaller appellations, such as some of the seventeen sub AVAs that exist within the Napa Valley AVA, InnoVint will now show the total that "rolls up" - into the larger appellation.

View this "roll-up" on the Lot details page > Composition tab, or export it by clicking on the new "export percentages" button. Get more details on how this works [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/appellation-roll-up?hsLang=en)!

Not in California, and interested? Let us know!

![Appellation Rollup](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Appellation%20Rollup.png?width=688&height=296&name=Appellation%20Rollup.png)

Bonus! We've also cleaned up the Lot Composition percentages.  Now, insignificant blend component amounts (<0.001%)  are combined into an 'Other' category on screen.  This change extends to [Blend Trials](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/blend-trials?hsLang=en) to make for a tidier page!

#### **FINANCE & Reporting usability improvements**

**More data on the Fruit Cost Worksheet**

They've always been on the export, but now you can gain instant context with block and vineyard tags now visible onscreen—enabling better sorting, and streamlining cost application.
![Product Update_Fruit Cost WS](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Product%20Update_Fruit%20Cost%20WS.png?width=688&height=138&name=Product%20Update_Fruit%20Cost%20WS.png)

Did you know? The search field on the Fruit Cost Worksheet matches text from the block name, vineyard name, grower, variety and also block and vineyard tags!

**Date range filter added to Cost Item Report**

When you are reconciling your indirect costs, try out the new date filter on the Cost Item Report to easily filter for one or more specific cost categories within the desired period.

**![Product Update_Cost Item filter](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Product%20Update_Cost%20Item%20filter.png?width=688&height=244&name=Product%20Update_Cost%20Item%20filter.png)**

**Cost Audit Report now includes Lot name, owner, bond, and tags columns**

To allow greater flexibility and filter capability when reconciling, new columns give you the flexibility to slice and dice data for targeted cost analysis. You can now find the lot name, bond, owner and tags on the Cost Audit Report export.

**Bottling Report updated for multiple bonds and owners**

Track bottling across bonds and owners with ease! The [Bottling Report](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000028666-bottling-report?hsLang=en) export now includes the bond (associated to the bulk and bottled lot) and owner (of both the Juice/Wine Lot and Case Good lot) to make it easier to report on specific locations and owners.

**More data in the Vessel at Point in Time Report**

As we move to improve the Vessel at Point in Time Report, you can now access additional vessel details within that report.  Now, find columns for:

- Date of Vessel Creation: know how long you've been storing that barrels!
- Lot Owner: filter for vessels containing wine for a specific owner, or check for lot/vessel owner mismatches!
- Vessel Type: Just reviewing tank fills or barrel usage? Use the new vessel type column

### Making things more efficient

#### Save a click! Quicker work order printing

Access work orders immediately after creation from the new confirmation message link.

![image-png-Jun-12-2025-06-38-26-5279-PM](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-12-2025-06-38-26-5279-PM.png?width=510&height=104&name=image-png-Jun-12-2025-06-38-26-5279-PM.png)

#### Tank Maps - Save X-Ray view option with camera settings

We've refreshed the camera setting function in [Tank Maps](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/tank-maps?hsLang=en#Save_View), and we've made it easier and more intuitive to create new positions, and edit/delete or favorite existing positions.

Even cooler, you can now save a camera position in X-Ray mode!

![Tank Maps Edit X-Ray](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Tank%20Maps%20Edit%20X-Ray.png?width=602&height=339&name=Tank%20Maps%20Edit%20X-Ray.png)

#### Bill of Lading usability improvements

Reducing manual steps: we made a few small changes to BOL functionality:

- Let's be honest... on all BOLs, the radio button for "Contains sulfites" now defaults to "Yes"
- When you perform a Remove Taxpaid (Case Goods) action, and generate a BOL, the radio option now defaults to "Taxpaid = Yes"
- When you generate a BOL for Case Goods that have contents, the BOL will automatically populate the case count

#### New Lab Source for Washington State!

If you work with our partner, Columbia Valley Wine Lab, you can now select that lab as your analysis source.

![Screenshot 2025-06-11 at 4.45.15 PM](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Screenshot%202025-06-11%20at%204.45.15%20PM.png?width=200&height=56&name=Screenshot%202025-06-11%20at%204.45.15%20PM.png)

### Bug Fixes

- Analysis Panel Issues: The temperature unit (i.e. F) in template panels was not respecting last temperature unit used and changing to celsius. Your preferred unit now sticks—ensuring consistency and avoiding confusion

### SUPPLY

Get full visibility and tighter control of your in-bond and taxpaid goods. We released our brand new case goods inventory management solution, [SUPPLY](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/supply?hsLang=en)!

See each SKU across locations and tax statuses in one place. Easily update inventory line items, reconcile and complete compliance reporting on your in-bond inventory for Section B of the TTB Report.

Using Commerce7? Synch inventory from SUPPLY to C7 in real time - depletions from C7 update every five minutes.
