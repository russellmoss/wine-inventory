### MAKE

#### Edit more easily!

- Forgot to backdate into last month?  We now support date edits on actions! Read more about it [here](/hc/en-us/articles/208141233-how-to-edit-or-delete-recorded-actions#edit-date).
  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-01-08-09-8594-PM.png?width=670&height=346&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-01-08-09-8594-PM.png)
- We added the ability to edit the "Amount Removed" from additives on submitted addition actions. This improves your ability to deplete your on-hand additives correctly when editing these actions. Find out more about editing Addition actions [here](/hc/en-us/articles/204321939-how-to-record-an-addition-additive-batch-tracker-add-on-#edit-action).

#### Improved reporting and filtering capability

We've made improvements to multiple reports and filters to make your lives easier!

- The **[Lot Explorer](/hc/en-us/lot-explorer#export) > Export by components** now includes two new columns — Tags (vineyard) and Tags (block). These additions can help to support organic or sustainability tracking for lots when you are using vineyard and block tags: to more easily total the percentage of compliant lots across your winery.
  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-10-28-31-3468-PM.png?width=670&height=321&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-10-28-31-3468-PM.png)
- Lot Explorer filters: We improved the Vessel Type filter and pinned commonly used vessels types (tanks and barrels) to the top. Want to create a lot view that is filtered by vessel type? Find out how using the [Lot Explorer layouts here](/hc/en-us/lot-explorer?hsLang=en).

  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-29-2026-08-08-47-1773-PM.png?width=316&height=254&name=image-png-Jun-29-2026-08-08-47-1773-PM.png)
- Looking for a specific Lot Property change? We added filters to our **[Lot Properties History Report](/hc/en-us/lot-properties-history-report?hsLang=en)** for an easier audit trail!  You can review changes to Lot code, name, color, style, stage, expected yield, tax class and archived status.  If you are using [Custom Lot Attributes](/hc/en-us/custom-attributes-lots?hsLang=en), you can see those changes too!

  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-11-28-06-1068-PM.png?width=670&height=187&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-11-28-06-1068-PM.png)
- Searching for lots without work orders, or trying to easily hover over the icons in the Lot Explorer to see what work has been assigned? The Work Order column header sort caret now sorts lots for work orders in the Lot Explorer
  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-11-37-36-9944-PM.png?width=670&height=310&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-11-37-36-9944-PM.png)
- We added an email column to the Grower Explorer export
- We now support multi-select for bonds in the work order explorer bond filter
- It's now easier for users with access to multiple owner tags to distinguish fruit costs on the Fruit Cost Report by adding a column for Owner tags on the export.

#### Blend Trials updates

- The Blend Trials export now includes the calculated composition percentages. This allows you to export and capture your blend trial at a specific point in time for historical records.
  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-09-51-07-5031-PM.png?width=670&height=167&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-09-51-07-5031-PM.png)
- ***Permissions update*!**

  - For wineries with owner tags, users with access to **Everything in winery** now have access to Blend Trials, including admins, team members, team members cannot submit, and read-only.  Read-only can view, but will not be able to create or edit Blend Trials.
  - Blend Trials are still not available for users accessing a winery with owner permissions. Find all about Blend Trials [here.](/hc/en-us/blend-trials?hsLang=en)
  - Blend Trials are available to admins, team members, team members cannot submit, and read-only for wineries without owner permissions. Read-only can view, but will not be able to create or edit Blend Trials.

- Use oak adjuncts?  We added Oak as product type, so you can more easily organize, track and select your chips, staves, blocks, and other types from your Additive Explorer.
  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-09-30-16-8110-PM.png?width=670&height=183&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-09-30-16-8110-PM.png)
- Truing up your on-hand dry goods for the end of the month? We've made it easier to find and [edit Batch Adjustments](https://app.hubspot.com/knowledge-article/6865708/editor/199615602795/content?redirectUrl=/knowledge/6865708/199604689011/articles/state/all) via the Batch details page.
- Metric? There is a new default addition rate available: mL/tonne.

### SUPPLY

#### Create Bills of Lading

You can now create Bills of Lading in SUPPLY!  Find the Create Bill of Lading option on any submitted movement or depletion action, or on open depletions.  You can also create BOLs outside of actions in the Reporting explorer! Find out more in [our Support Article!](/hc/en-us/how-to-create-a-bill-of-lading-bol-in-supply?hsLang=en)

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-25-2026-12-21-51-7036-AM.png?width=670&height=234&name=image-png-Jun-25-2026-12-21-51-7036-AM.png)

#### **Explorer and action improvements**

- Notes on actions now export with the Action History Feed exports
- We added a [change column to the Reconcile action](/hc/en-us/how-to-reconcile-inventory#change) to help you understand the variance on each reconciliation action

  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-25-2026-12-23-54-9761-AM.png?width=670&height=301&name=image-png-Jun-25-2026-12-23-54-9761-AM.png)

#### **SUPPLY now creates reconciliation actions from Commerce7 inventory resets!**

**All** inventory adjustments and movements in Commerce7 now create an appropriate action in SUPPLY.

A Reconcile action is created in SUPPLY for all of the following transactions in C7:

- 'Reset inventory' for a specific C7 product at a specific location
- 'Edit Inventory Count' bulk inventory reset on the Inventory page
- 'Import inventory' from a csv to bulk update inventory counts

Read all about the [C7 - SUPPLY integration](/hc/en-us/supply-commerce7-integration?hsLang=en) here!
