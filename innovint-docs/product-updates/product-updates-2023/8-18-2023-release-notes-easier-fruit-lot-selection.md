# 8/18/2023 Release Notes: Easier Fruit Lot selection

Release Notes from August 18, 2023 include:

### Improvements

#### New Fruit Lot Picker for the Receive Fruit action

For our Harvest preppers and high throughput users, we added our standard lot picker slideover to the Receive Fruit action for ease of selecting your fruit lots.

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Aug-18-2023-06-50-24-1311-PM.png?width=554&height=175&name=image-png-Aug-18-2023-06-50-24-1311-PM.png)

Using the lot picker, you can now filter by variety, vineyard, appellation, tag or owner as you normally would elsewhere in InnoVint.

![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Aug-18-2023-06-51-48-2335-PM.png)

#### Logging in and changing passwords is easier

Did you really forget your password, or are you just typing with capslock on?? You now have the option to "Show Password" when logging in or resetting your password. Click on the eye icon to show or hide the password.

Find out more about other changes to current password guidelines [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/password-guidelines?hsLang=en) (hint: it no longer has to be 16 characters!)

![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Aug-22-2023-05-31-05-9464-PM.png)

#### Have a lot of contracts?

Expect improved loading time for your Contracts Explorer due to recent performance improvements!

![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Aug-22-2023-07-27-28-1059-PM.png)

#### BarrelWise is now included as an analysis source

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Aug-18-2023-07-09-28-1869-PM.png?width=77&height=100&name=image-png-Aug-18-2023-07-09-28-1869-PM.png)Have a high tech cellar? You can now select BarrelWise as an analysis source and see the logo on any BarrelWise sourced analysis results:

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Aug-17-2023-11-04-26-8383-PM.png?width=376&height=184&name=image-png-Aug-17-2023-11-04-26-8383-PM.png)

#### InnoFlex Multi Winery Lot Explorer now includes percentage columns for vintage, variety and appellation

We've add additional vintage, appellation and variety percentage columns so you can filter, sort and view lots that meet specific thresholds.

![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Aug-22-2023-05-27-43-7522-PM.png)

![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Aug-22-2023-05-23-49-1547-PM.png)

### Bugs

This is a bit embarrassing, but we wanted to let you know about a few pesky issues that made their way into the app, and that we've recently fixed.

Make sure you refresh your browsers on a consistent basis to get any updates to InnoVint as they are released!

- Were you seeing too many Oops! error messages?
  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Aug-17-2023-04-20-33-1921-PM.png?width=316&height=68&name=image-png-Aug-17-2023-04-20-33-1921-PM.png)
  We had a glitch where all error messages lost useful pointers to help you fix your actions.  Thank you all for so patiently writing into Support for help!
- The Dashboard was temporarily missing data in certain "cards", such as Open Work Orders and Barrel Availability.
- Add Note button was non responsive when creating a new Contract.
- Were you trying to update your old harvest work orders?  Your work order template edits should now save as expected.
- Our Bottling Report export was duplicating lines when owner or bond filters were utilized.
