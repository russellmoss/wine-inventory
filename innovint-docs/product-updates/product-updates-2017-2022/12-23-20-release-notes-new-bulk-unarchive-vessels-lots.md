# 12/23/20 Release Notes - New!! Bulk Unarchive Vessels & Lots

The software release for December 23, 2020 includes:

### Improvement:

### Bulk Unarchive Vessels

In your **Vessel Explorer,** you will find an updated *Archive or unarchive vessels* option from the Manage vessels button. From here you can update the vessels in bulk.

### Bulk Unarchive Lots

In your **Lot Explorer,** you will find an updated *Archive or unarchive lots* option from the Manage lots button. From here you can update the lots in bulk.
