# 9/24/21 Release Notes - Press Yields Breakdown, Receive Dry Good Actions in WAF & More!

The software releases for September 24, 2021 include:

### Improvements

#### Press Yields in Process Fruit to Volume and Drain and Press

Yield is now displayed per lot for Process Fruit to Volume actions and per press fraction for Drain and Press actions.

Process Fruit to Volume summary: ![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Sep-22-2021-03-28-40-82-PM.png)

#### Winery Activity Feed

Winery Activity Feed now filterable for Receive Dry Good actions and displays Product name and Batch name along with amount received  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Sep-22-2021-03-13-17-92-PM.png)

#### Other Improvements

- **Inventory at Point in Time** report includes additional fields in export, including alcohol % at point in time.
  *\*\*\*CA wineries - use this to determine under/over 14% tax class for the CDTFA state reporting!*
- New warning when blending across owners (if activated) ![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/Screen%20Shot%202021-09-21%20at%203-54-51%20PM-png.png)

- Generate Bill of Lading button from within submitted B2B action working
