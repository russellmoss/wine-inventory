# 8/23/19 - Harvest Launchpad, Ribbon Label Printing, Bulk download weigh tags, and MORE!

The Software Release on 8/23/19 includes:

### New Features

- [Harvest Launchpad](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/360033363671-harvest-launchpad?hsLang=en)

The Advanced Vineyard Tracking add-on feature now includes the NEW! Harvest Launchpad. This interface simplifies crushpad operations with a single view for all scheduled, incoming fruit. It provides the necessary details of each receive fruit task, direct, single-click access to the work orders, as well as a quick link to receive unscheduled fruit.

- [Ribbon Label Printing](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/115000605043-which-labels-are-recommended-and-where-can-i-purchase-them-?hsLang=en#ribbon) *(released prior to 8/23/19)*

Now you can print your Lot and Vessel labels using a ribbon label printer, like a Dymo or Zebra printer. In additional to our sheet labels, InnoVint can now generate 2 different label sizes for dedicated label printers, which can be downloaded and sent to your preferred printer.

- [Bulk download weigh tags](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/205606335-fruit-intake-report?hsLang=en#weightag)

Download your weigh tags in bulk from within the Fruit Intake Report. Use the filters at the top of the report to refine your selection.

### Improvements

- [Bulk edit lot stage](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/204339859-how-to-change-lot-stage?hsLang=en#bulk)
- [Bulk edit vessel attributes](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/206240653-change-or-edit-vessel-details?hsLang=en#bulk)
- [Sugar reading in Receive Fruit action](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/360005125552-receive-fruit?hsLang=en)

CA Winemakers! The new Sugar Reading option, now included as part of a Receive Fruit action or task, allows you to enter the brix of your fruit at harvest. It is optional, and can also be added, deleted or edited *AFTER* the Receive Fruit action has been recorded. This value will populate the [Fruit Intake Report](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/205606335-fruit-intake-report?hsLang=en) as the Last Brix on the fruit lot to help you with the CA Grape Crush Report.

- [Harvest Calendar](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/115001093751-harvest-calendar?hsLang=en)

New improvements now show block and varietal information (previously fruit lot code), as well as the Owner tag (only with custom crush permissions activated) and aggregated total tonnage per day.

- [Update to Drain and Press actions and tasks](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/205552639-drain-and-press?hsLang=en)

New flow requires selection to separate or combine press fractions before selecting lots and vessels. Previously the selection defaulted to separate press fractions and users might have proceeded to select a lot and vessels before realizing that they meant to combine the press fractions, thus losing the information that was already entered.

- New look for the Lot Details page!
