# 04/13/17: ETS Labs Integration

### New Feature Announcement:

- **ETS Laboratories Integration** - automatically import and associate analytical sample results with lots and vessels in InnoVint

### Direct Integration with ETS Labs

Are you frequently sending samples to ETS Laboratories to test your juice or wine? Would you like for InnoVint to import these results as soon as they are available from ETS? If so, today is your lucky day! This deep integration with ETS has been released to clients.

**How does it work?**This integration allows InnoVint clients to print sample stickers from inside our platform. Once the results are posted by ETS, InnoVint automatically pulls and associates the data to your lots and/or vessels. No data entry required! Learn more about [ETS Integration](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/115001540266-ets-integration-overview?hsLang=en) in our Online Help Center.

***This feature requires activation.*** If you are interested please contact support@innovint.us.
