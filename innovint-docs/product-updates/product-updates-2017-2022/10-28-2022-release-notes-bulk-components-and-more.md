# 10/28/2022 Release Notes: Bulk Components and more!

Release notes from October 28 2022 include:

### New Features

#### Bulk Wine Component flexibility!

You can now create a new lot composition more easily for bulk wines, without requiring vineyard and block attributes. Check out the new workflow [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/how-to-enter-bulk-wine-and-unknown-lot-composition?hsLang=en)!

![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Oct-19-2022-09-51-01-67-PM.png)

#### Change your costing currency!

InnoVint now supports most costing currencies including the Australian and New Zealand dollars, the Euro, the British Pound, the Chilean Peso, the South African Rand and more! Just reach out to our support team at support@innovint.us to find out more.

### Improvements

#### Lot Explorer (Multi-winery) layout improvements

You can set your favorite default layout (specific per user), and rename or delete saved layouts in your Settings. Use this new feature to the max and save the reports that YOU want to see!

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Oct-19-2022-10-08-49-74-PM.png?width=657&height=254&name=image-png-Oct-19-2022-10-08-49-74-PM.png)

#### Search bar now shows "archived" tag

If your search results include archived items, you can now identify them easily in the winery search feature.

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Oct-20-2022-11-15-16-24-PM.png?width=357&height=257&name=image-png-Oct-20-2022-11-15-16-24-PM.png)

#### Archive Vessels within a Bond-to-Bond transfer out

When you ship out lots, you now have the option to automatically archive their vessels so you don't need to go back and archive barrels that have left your facility!

![](https://support.innovint.us/hubfs/image-png-Oct-20-2022-11-21-27-41-PM.png)

#### Cost Item backdate time is now 11.59pm

When you add a cost item and choose to backdate it, the time will now default to 11.59pm.

### Bug Fixes

- Work Order Template "Unable to load" error
- Unable to archive or unarchive blocks
