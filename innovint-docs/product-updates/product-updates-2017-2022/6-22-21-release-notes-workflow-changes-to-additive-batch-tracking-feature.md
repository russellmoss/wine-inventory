# 6/22/21 Release Notes - Workflow changes to Additive Batch Tracking feature

The software releases for June 22, 2021 include:

### Improvements:

#### Additives Explorer

- New way to receive additives! Utilize the "+ Receive Additive" slide over to input new additive types and batches as well as receive more inventory.
  ![Screen_Shot_2021-06-03_at_8_56_12_AM_1.png](https://innovint.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4402369043469/Screen_Shot_2021-06-03_at_8_56_12_AM_1.png)
- Add new additive products and new batches from within the Receive Additive slide over.
  ![Screen_Shot_2021-06-03_at_9_17_07_AM.png](https://innovint.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4402362531725/Screen_Shot_2021-06-03_at_9_17_07_AM.png)
- Find a new action "Receive Dry Good" action in the batch history
- Notes now available on Product details, Batch details, and Receive Dry Good actions.
  ![Screen_Shot_2021-06-07_at_9_49_41_AM.png](https://innovint.zendesk.com/hc/article_attachments/4402568414477/Screen_Shot_2021-06-07_at_9_49_41_AM.png)
- See more details in the [Additive Batch Tracking & Addition Calculator](https://innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/115000825066-how-to-create-additives-and-additive-batches?hsLang=en) section of our Online Support Center.
- Don't have our Additive Batch Tracking & Addition Calculator? Email us at [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us)to activate!

### Bug Fixes

- Inventory at Point in Time Report totals & units
- Lot cost tab showing correct finished goods cost
- B2B to another InnoVint winery owners
- iPad top navigation bar
