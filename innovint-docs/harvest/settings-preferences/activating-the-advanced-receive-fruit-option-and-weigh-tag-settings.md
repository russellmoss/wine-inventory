# Activating the Advanced Receive Fruit option and Weigh Tag Settings

InnoVint's Advanced Receive Fruit option allows you to generate weighmaster certificates (or weigh tags) within InnoVint. The weighmaster certificates are compliant with the regulations and requirements set forth by the [CDFA](https://www.cdfa.ca.gov/dms/programs/wm/wm.html) (California Department of Food and Agriculture) and [Napa County](https://www.countyofnapa.org/1635/Weighmaster-Enforcement).

For a side-by-side comparison of InnoVint's **Simple vs Advanced Receive Fruit** options, see [this article](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/115003754563-simple-vs-advanced-receive-fruit?hsLang=en) in the Support Center.

### **How to activate the Advanced Receive Fruit option:**

### Activating the Advanced Receive Fruit option and Weigh Tag Settings-order

1. Turn on the feature by selecting "Enable advanced receive fruit system."![Activating the Advanced Receive Fruit option and Weigh Tag Settings-settings](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-settings.webp?width=688&height=122&name=Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-settings.webp)

   If you use metric (tonnes or kilograms) as your weight unit, you will not see this option in your Harvest settings. Please contact us directly at [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us) to activate the feature!
2. Next, review the starting weigh tag number. Weigh tags will begin at this number and increase consecutively for each Receive Fruit action. Each weigh tag sequence will be specific to the selected weighing location.  The first weigh tag number must be greater than all previous weigh tag numbers generated by InnoVint.
   If you are just getting started with weigh tags, please reach out to [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us) to set your first number.
3. If you have already created Receive Fruit work orders before activating Advanced Receive Fruit, you will see an error. Please **delete** any open work orders created prior to activation, as you will not be able to create a weigh tag from Receive Fruit tasks created and submitted prior to activation.
   ![image-png-Jun-12-2025-04-13-55-3612-PM](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-12-2025-04-13-55-3612-PM.png?width=429&height=141&name=image-png-Jun-12-2025-04-13-55-3612-PM.png)
4. You will now see a new tab in your Settings, underneath Harvest: **Weigh tag settings**
   ![Activating the Advanced Receive Fruit option and Weigh Tag Settings-tab](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-tab.webp?width=175&height=82&name=Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-tab.webp)

### Additional Weigh Tag Settings

In the Weigh tag settings tab you will be able to access and edit the following information:

- - **Weighing location:** This will populate automatically if you have a single weighing location, and will be a dropdown menu if you have [multiple weighing locations](#MWL). The first weigh tag number, Weighmaster and Weighing address are specific to the selected location.
  - **First weigh tag number:** This field displays your starting weigh tag number for each location.
  - **Weighmaster/Weighing address**: The name and address of the weighing facility where the scale is registered.
  - **Weighed for**: InnoVint auto-populates this field on the weigh tag based on the bond name and address linked to the fruit lots received. *To update the address for a bond please submit a request to InnoVint Support via the link in the upper right corner of the Support Center. Subject: Update bond information*
  - **Deputy weighmasters**: Name(s) of person(s) onsite registered as Deputy Weighmaster(s). Add or archive your Deputy Weighmasters here.
  - **Tare containers**: The tare weight of one or more weigh groups. Enter your commonly received tare weights here. *Example: An individual MacroBin at 89.5 lbs or two (2) MacroBins at 89.5 lbs each*.
    - For California wineries, be sure to weigh your bins with a Deputy Weighmaster and submit your [Common Tare Notice paperwork](https://www.cdfa.ca.gov/dms/programs/wm/CommonTareBrochure.pdf). This allows you to include the Common Tare Code Designation when adding Tare containers to InnoVint  ![Activating the Advanced Receive Fruit option and Weigh Tag Settings-container](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-container.webp?width=688&height=380&name=Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-container.webp)

**Note**: In the Receive Fruit action or work order task, you will have the additional option of "Custom Container" to record the tare measurement of trucks or trailers, or non standard tare containers that you may receive.

### Multiple Weighing Locations

If you have activated Advanced Receive Fruit, you will need to contact InnoVint Support to create additional weighing locations.  Once created, the Weigh tag settings screen will display the weighing locations dropdown.

You may edit the weighmaster and weighing address for all locations here in Weigh tag settings.

![Activating the Advanced Receive Fruit option and Weigh Tag Settings-location](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-location.webp?width=688&height=304&name=Activating%20the%20Advanced%20Receive%20Fruit%20option%20and%20Weigh%20Tag%20Settings-location.webp)
**What is specific to a weighing location?** You must set these for each unique weighing location:

- First weigh tag number
- Weighmaster
- Weighing address

**What is not specific to a weighing location?** These will be the same across all weighing locations in your account:

- Deputy Weighmasters
- Tare containers
- Owner tags

### **How to Deactivate the Advanced Receive Fruit option:**

If you decide that you'd rather use the Simple Receive Fruit action, contact InnoVint Support by submitting a ticket from within the Support Center, link located in the upper right corner. *Subject: Deactivate Advanced Receive Fruit*

**Note**: Only account *Admins* can activate or request to deactivate the Advanced Receive Fruit option.

### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

**Q: Is there an additional fee to activate the Advanced Receive Fruit option?**

*If you do not see the option to activate the Advanced Receive Fruit option in your Settings, please contact InnoVint Support using the "Submit help ticket" link at the top right, or email us at [support@innovint.us.](mailto:support@innovint.us)*

**Q: I started harvest with the Simple Receive Fruit option, and then activated the Advanced Receive Fruit option. Can I print weigh tags for the fruit that was received via the simple option?**

*A: No. Weigh tags cannot be generated for any fruit that was received via the Simple Receive Fruit option. Please reach out to InnoVint Support for advice if you want to activate Advanced Receive Fruit after beginning fruit intake.*

**Q: I don't see the Advanced Receive Fruit option in my harvest settings. Do I have this option?**

*A: You may not see the option to activate Advanced Receive Fruit in your Settings if you use a metric weight unit such as tonnes or kilograms.  Please reach out to [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us) and we can turn on Advanced Receive Fruit for you!*

**Q: I don't see Multiple Weighing Locations as an option in my harvest settings. Do I have this option?**

*A: Please reach out to [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us) and we can  verify your subscription and set up  Multiple Weighing Locations for you!  Please provide the following for each location:*

- *Location Name (this will populate the Weighing Location dropdown menu)*
- *First Weigh tag number*
- *Weighmaster (this will print on the weigh tag)*
- *Weighing Address (this will print on the weigh tag)*
