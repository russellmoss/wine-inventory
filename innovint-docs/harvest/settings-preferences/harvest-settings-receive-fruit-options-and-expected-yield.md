# Harvest Settings (Receive Fruit options and Expected Yield)

Before harvest begins, take a few minutes to review your Harvest Settings in InnoVint. These settings control:

- [How you receive fruit (Simple vs. Advanced)](#receivefruit)
- [Expected yield per varietal](#settingexpyield)
- [Fermentation Management Worksheet stages](#lot-stages)

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-25-2026-12-01-43-0804-AM.png?width=670&height=313&name=image-png-Jun-25-2026-12-01-43-0804-AM.png)

### Choose Your Receive Fruit Option

InnoVint offers two ways to record fruit receipts:

- **Simple**

  - No weigh tags generated
  - Enter net weight only
  - The default setting for all new winery accounts
- **Advanced**

  - Automatic weigh tag generation
  - Enter individual scale readings to calculate gross, tare, and net weights
  - Winery admins must activate this setting

To switch to **Advanced**:

1. Click Activate Advanced Receive Fruit and review your starting weigh tag number.

   1. Metric accounts will not see this option in Settings, and must contact support@innovint.us for activation.
2. A new Weigh tag setting option will appear under Harvest Settings in the left hand menu, where you can manage the weigh tag details that auto-populate during fruit receiving.

For more detailed information on **Simple vs Advanced Receive Fruit** options, see [this article](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115003754563-simple-vs-advanced-receive-fruit?hsLang=en) in the Support Center.

### Setting the Expected Yield

InnoVint allows you to set an Expected Yield per weight unit for each variety. If you do not set a yield, InnoVint uses [these default yields.](#default)

The expected yield estimates the volume of lots (fruit lots and juice/wine lots) tracked in weight for the purposes of calculating volume-based additive rates and estimating future lot volume.

To set the expected yield for each varietal in **Harvest Settings**:

1. Click **+Add varietal** and select from the drop-down list.
2. Enter the expected yield per your winery’s chosen weight and volume units.
3. Click **Save** before leaving the page.

- - To remove a varietal, click **Delete** at the far right.
  - These values apply **only to new lots**. Existing lots won’t update.

![Harvest Settings (Receive Fruit options and Expected Yield)-exp yield](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Harvest%20Settings%20(Receive%20Fruit%20options%20and%20Expected%20Yield)-exp%20yield.webp?width=670&height=175&name=Harvest%20Settings%20(Receive%20Fruit%20options%20and%20Expected%20Yield)-exp%20yield.webp)

InnoVint also allows you to set the expected yield *per lot*. See [**this article**](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/community/posts/360014610111-how-to-adjust-the-expected-yield-of-a-lot?hsLang=en)  for instructions on how to set the expected yield for a specific fruit lot during fruit lot creation.

#### If no yield is set, InnoVint defaults to:

- 150 gal/ton
- 165 gal/tonne
- 0.165 gal/kg
- 570 liters/ton
- 628 liters/tonne
- 0.628 liters/kg
- 5.7 hl/ton
- 6.28 hl/tonne
- 0.00628 hl/kg

### Fermentation Management Worksheet stages

By default, [Fermentation Worksheets](/hc/en-us/articles/360015385351-using-the-fermentation-worksheets-aka-ferm-gen-?hsLang=en) include lots in the **Processed, Fermenting, Settling** and **Cold Soak** stages. These are generally the stages your lots are in over the peak of harvest, and we recommend using this default for assigning and tracking harvest analysis tasks.

However, you can choose to view lots in different stages by updating the selected Fermentation Management Worksheet stages.  Just select the [lot stages](/hc/en-us/articles/204339859-how-to-change-lot-stage?hsLang=en) you want included (add in Extended Maceration, or ML over harvest - or start tracking only your ML lots after your lots are through primary) and click Save worksheet stages!
