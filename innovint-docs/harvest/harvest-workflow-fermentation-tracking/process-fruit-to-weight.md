# Process Fruit to Weight

The Process Fruit action is the second step (after Receive Fruit) in bringing fruit into your facility during harvest and allows for a variety of options and flexibility in how to get your fruit from the crush pad to your vessels.

Process Fruit to *Weight* is specific for fruit that will be sent to your vessels with skins and tracked in weight (i.e. tons, tonnes, or kgs), to be later drained and pressed. If you want to track your lot in volume (i.e. gallons or liters), please see more about the Process to Volume action [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360006828911-process-fruit-to-volume?hsLang=en).

This action can only be performed on Fruit Lots with contents (via Receive Fruit action) and captures information on what and how much fruit you are processing, optional process steps (e.g. sorting, destemming, etc.), and the lot(s) and vessel(s) that you are sending your fruit to.

### via Direct Action

1. **Received fruit to process**
   - Click on **Select lots in picker** to select one or more Fruit lots to process at the same time
   - Enter the weight to process for each Fruit lot. This can be all or part of the total weight of the unprocessed fruit.![Process Fruit to Weight-direct enter weight](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-direct%20enter%20weight.webp?width=670&height=120&name=Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-direct%20enter%20weight.webp)
2. **Process** (optional)
   - Enter each step. Instructions are also optional.
   - Click on +Add step for more![Process Fruit to Weight-direct add step](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-direct%20add%20step.webp?width=670&height=108&name=Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-direct%20add%20step.webp)
3. **Process to**
   - Lot: Select or create your destination lot
     - If you choose to create a new lot, make sure to double check your [expected yield](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/community/posts/360014610111-how-to-adjust-the-expected-yield-of-a-lot?hsLang=en). This number will determine your additive calculations for the time your lot is measured in weight (i.e. until Drain and Press).                                                   ![Process Fruit to Weight-expected yield](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-expected%20yield.webp?width=266&height=354&name=Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-expected%20yield.webp)
   - Vessels: Select one or more destination vessels and enter the weight
   - Lot Stage: Will default to *Processed*.
   - Click on **+ Add Lot** to add more lots![Process Fruit to Weight-direct add lot](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-direct%20add%20lot.webp?width=670&height=300&name=Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-direct%20add%20lot.webp)
4. **Archiving**: Leave the box checked to archive empty Fruit lots. Uncheck this box if you expect to receive fruit in the future for a Fruit lot that is being emptied of its current contents.
5. **Summary**: Double check the weight processed from your Fruit lot(s) and the total amount that is processed to your Juice lot
6. Click on ![Process Fruit to Weight-record button](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-record%20button.webp?width=133&height=26&name=Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-record%20button.webp)

   - Click on **Select lots in picker** to choose one or more lots to process at the same time

   - Enter each step. Instructions are also optional.

   - Click on **+ Add lot** to add more lots![Process Fruit to Weight-add lot](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-add%20lot.webp?width=670&height=203&name=Process%20Fruit%20to%20Weight-add%20lot.webp)
4. **Archiving**: Leave the box checked to archive empty Fruit lots. Uncheck this box if you expect to receive fruit in the future for a Fruit lot that is being emptied of its current contents.

### FAQ

#### Q: Can I process more than one Fruit lot at once?

*A: Yes. Go to the **Received fruit to process** step and click on Edit lots to choose additional Fruit lots.*

#### Q: Can I process a Fruit lot into more than one destination lot?

*A: Yes. In the **Received fruit to process** step, enter the weight you'd like to process in total. This can be all or part of the entire lot fill. Then in the **Process to** step, select +Add lot to add more destination lots. Select one or more vessels per lot, and enter the fill for each. Make sure to double check your final **Summary**to make sure everything balances (i.e. the **processed** weight is equal to the **processed to** tonnage.)*

*If you process into multiple lots, please note that the fruit lot composition will be the same across all lots and vessels. If you want to distribute different fruit lots into distinct lot compositions, you must use one Process action/task per unique lot.*

#### Q: Can I process fruit into a lot or vessel that already has contents?

*A:Yes. In the **Process to** step, choose 'Combine with existing lot' and select the lot from your list. Then select a vessel, empty or with contents, and enter how many tons were added to calculate the ending fill. You CANNOT add weight to a lot or vessel that currently has a fill measured in volume (i.e. you won't be able to add tons to a lot that is measured in gallons.)*

#### **Q: I'm getting a Negative Fruit Weight Error, what gives?**

*A: Check the Received Fruit to Process section of the action or task.  Here, InnoVint will calculate and display how much of the fruit lot weight is left remaining.  If you've entered more weight than you have in the fruit lot, you will see a Negative Fruit Weight error message when you try to submit the action.  Correct the processed weight to be less than or equal to the starting fruit weight.*
