# How to track vineyard data in Fruit lots

This article covers how to track vineyard data in accounts *without* the GROW module (formerly known as Advanced Vineyard Tracking). For more information on tracking vineyard data by block with GROW activated, see [this article](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360026806092-how-to-record-and-track-data-and-analyses-on-vineyard-blocks?hsLang=en) in our Support Center.

For those without GROW, vineyard data and maturity analytics can also be recorded and tracked on Fruit lots.

Depending on how you sample and track your vineyard data, fruit lots can be representative of an entire block or multiple picks from the same block.

This article covers:

- How to track vineyard sampling data via:
  - [Direct action](#direct-action)
    - [Analysis](#Analysis-direct-action)
    - [Analysis Import](#analysis-import)
  - [Work orders](#work-order)
  - [InnoApp](#App)
- How to track vineyard data via:
  - [Custom Actions on Fruit Lots](#custom-action)
  - [Notes](#record-note)

#### **Work Orders**

Create a new work order for Fruit lot analysis:

- In the top Nav bar via the Create work order button/menu
- In the Work Order Explorer, via the +Create work order button
- In the Vineyard OR Fruit Lot Explorers, via the blue Create work order button
  ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-create wo](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-create%20wo.webp?width=688&height=46&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-create%20wo.webp)

Set up your analysis task:

- Click on +Add task and select *Analysis* (Analysis Import is not available as a task)
- Select your analysis types and run on lot composite
- Select your fruit lots
- Don't forget to give your work order a title and assign it to a winery member before you click on ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-create wo button](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-create%20wo%20button.webp?width=117&height=26&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-create%20wo%20button.webp)
- Find out more detailed information on using work orders [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360050001811-using-work-orders-in-innovint?hsLang=en).

#### InnoApp

Using the InnoApp, you can record analysis from right in the vineyard, even offline!

- Open the InnoApp on your device
- Search for or select your fruit lot from the Lot list
  Tip: use the search field and enter "**FL**" to skip to your Fruit lots
- Click on **Record Action**
- Select *Analysis* from the menu, then choose Lot Composite and enter your analysis value(s)
- Click on **Record** at the bottom of the page
  ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-innoapp1](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-innoapp1.webp?width=168&height=244&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-innoapp1.webp)  ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-innoapp2](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-innoapp2.webp?width=139&height=247&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-innoapp2.webp)  ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-innoapp3](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-innoapp3.webp?width=138&height=248&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-innoapp3.webp)

### How to track vineyard data

If you  do not have the Grow module activated, which allows you to record specific [vineyard management events](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360026806092-how-to-record-and-track-data-and-analyses-on-vineyard-blocks?hsLang=en#event) by Block, such as Phenological Events (Germination of Cover Crop, Budbreak, Bloom, Fruit Set, Hard Seed, Veraison), Application Tracking (Irrigation, Spraying, Pruning, Thinning, Training, Additions/Treatments, Cover Crop/Weed Control, Fruit Drop) and Visual Observations (Water Status, Nutrient Status, Soil Conditions, Pest and Disease Monitoring), you can also record Fruit lot and Vineyard data with the following options:

#### Custom Actions on Fruit lots

If there is any data that you would like to record as part of your fruit lot history (e.g. canopy management, crop thinning, spraying, etc.), custom actions or tasks are your answer!

- Select *Custom Action* from the drop-down list for Direct actions, or add a Custom task to your work order.
- (optional) Change the title of your Custom Action/Task by clicking on the pencil to the right
- (optional) Add a text note
- Fill in the details
- Record your Custom Action, or create your Custom Task work order!

For more details on Custom Actions, see our Support Center article [here](//innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/204848455-using-a-custom-action-or-custom-task?hsLang=en). Custom Actions cannot be applied to Vineyards or Blocks.

- **On Fruit Lots**
  - Find all your Fruit lots in the Fruit Lot Explorer:
    ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-explorer](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-explorer.webp?width=688&height=105&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-explorer.webp)
  - Select your Fruit lot and from the Lot details Dashboard, click on  "**Add note.**"
    ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-add note](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-add%20note.webp?width=458&height=435&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-add%20note.webp)
- **On a Vineyard or Block**
  - From the Vineyard or Block details page, click on  "**Add note.**"
    ![How to track vineyard data in Fruit Lots-add note vineyard](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-add%20note%20vineyard.webp?width=279&height=444&name=How%20to%20track%20vineyard%20data%20in%20Fruit%20Lots-add%20note%20vineyard.webp)

Find out more about attaching photos or files to lots and vineyards on the desktop app [here.](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360009672091-how-to-add-a-photo-note?hsLang=en)

Find out how to add notes specifically via InnoApp [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360009672091-how-to-add-a-photo-note?hsLang=en#mobile).  Please note that you cannot add photo notes via InnoApp without the GROW module activated.
