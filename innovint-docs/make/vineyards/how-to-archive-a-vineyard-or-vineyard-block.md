# How to Archive a Vineyard or Vineyard Block

If one of your vineyards or a vineyard block is no longer in use, you can archive it to remove it from view. Please note, they can only be archived and not deleted.

1) Go into your Vineyard Explorer page and select the vineyard you want to archive

![How to Archive a Vineyard or Vineyard Block-explorer](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-explorer.png?width=250&height=310&name=How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-explorer.png)

2) Select the "More" drop-down in the upper right-hand corner. Click "Archive Vineyard"

![How to Archive a Vineyard or Vineyard Block-dropdown menu](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-dropdown%20menu.png?width=559&height=146&name=How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-dropdown%20menu.png)

3) You can do this for an individual block by clicking into the Block Details on the Vineyard Details Page

![How to Archive a Vineyard or Vineyard Block-indv block](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-indv%20block.webp?width=670&height=213&name=How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-indv%20block.webp)

4) Click the "More" drop-down in the upper right hand corner and select "Archive Block"

![How to Archive a Vineyard or Vineyard Block-more dropdown](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-more%20dropdown.webp?width=670&height=116&name=How%20to%20Archive%20a%20Vineyard%20or%20Vineyard%20Block-more%20dropdown.webp)

### FAQ

**Q. Will archiving a vineyard prevent the vineyard from displaying as part of a lot's composition?**

*No, archiving a vineyard will have no impact on a lot's composition involving that vineyard. The vineyard will still display as part of the lot composition.*
