# How to Delete or Archive Lots and Vessels

Lots, Vessels, Dry Goods and Vineyard sources may be archived in InnoVint for numerous reasons. Archiving an inventory item removes it from the active list in your explorers and pickers, and limits the actions and activities that may be recorded on the item. Archiving *does not* delete the lot, vessel, dry good or vineyard - or any history - from your account.

Lots, vessels, and dry goods can be deleted from InnoVint *only if they have no recorded actions on them*. If *any* action has ever been recorded, and even if all actions have been deleted and nothing shows in the lot, vessel, or batch history, you will not be able to delete one of these from your account. In that case, the best option is to archive.

### **How to archive a lot or vessel**

Lots and vessels can only be archived if they are empty (i.e. the current contents/fill = 0).

- InnoVint offers to auto-archive lots by default if an action or task is submitted that empties the lot (e.g. bottling, bond to bond transfer, process a fruit lot to ton/gallons, blending, etc.). Simply uncheck the this archiving box to keep the lot active after the contents has been removed:
  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jul-12-2021-04-04-57-55-PM.png?width=509&height=124&name=image-png-Jul-12-2021-04-04-57-55-PM.png)
- **To archive a *single* lot or vessel**Select the lot or vessel from the Lot or Vessel Explorer > click 'More' in the upper right > then 'Archive lot' or 'Archive vessel'
  ![How to Archive_Delete_single lot](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20Archive_Delete_single%20lot.png?width=670&height=144&name=How%20to%20Archive_Delete_single%20lot.png)
- **To bulk archive *multiple* lots or vessels**
  Go to the Lot or Vessel Explorer > click 'Manage lots (or vessels)' in the upper right > then 'Archive or unarchive lots (or vessels)'
  ![How to Archive_Delete_bulk](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20Archive_Delete_bulk.png?width=670&height=112&name=How%20to%20Archive_Delete_bulk.png)

You can also bulk archive or unarchive Fruit lots using the Manage lots button in the Lot Explorer - be sure to use the Fruit Lot filter in the Lot picker to display Fruit Lots. ![How to archive_delete_fruit lots bulk](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20archive_delete_fruit%20lots%20bulk.png?width=670&height=174&name=How%20to%20archive_delete_fruit%20lots%20bulk.png)

### **Where to view archived lots and vessels**

**Lot Explorer**

Go to the Lot Explorer and use the filter at the top, titled 'Archived' to open up the filter options.

The 'Archived' filter allows you to choose different options for displaying your lots. You can choose to:

- Leave the default view - displays only active lots
- **Show active and archived** **lots** - displays both archived and non-archived lots/vessels in the explorers
- **Show only archived** - displays only the lots you have flagged as 'Archived'

![Lot Explorer_Archived Filter](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Lot%20Explorer_Archived%20Filter.png?width=670&height=156&name=Lot%20Explorer_Archived%20Filter.png)

**Vessel Explorer**

The 'Hide archived' filter allows you to choose between 3 different options for displaying your archived lots. You can choose to:

- **Hide archived** - removes all archived lots/vessels from the lot/vessel explorer display
- **Show archived** - displays both archived and non-archived lots/vessels in the explorers
- **Show only archived** - displays only the lots you have flagged as 'Archived'

![Vessel Explorer_Archive Filter](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Vessel%20Explorer_Archive%20Filter.png?width=670&height=176&name=Vessel%20Explorer_Archive%20Filter.png)

**Note:** Once archived, no movement actions can be completed on the lot/vessel, no work order can be created, and the lot stage cannot be changed. You *will* be able to apply notes and record analysis.

### **How to unarchive a lot****or vessel**

Lots and vessels can only be unarchived if they are already archived.

- **To unarchive a *single* lot or vessel**
  Select the lot or vessel from the Lot or Vessel Explorer > click 'More' in the upper right > then 'Unarchive lot'![How to archive_delete_unarchive](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20archive_delete_unarchive.png?width=670&height=127&name=How%20to%20archive_delete_unarchive.png)
- **To bulk archive *multiple* lots or vessels**
  Go to the Lot Explorer or Vessel Explorer > click 'Manage lots (or vessels)' in the upper right > then 'Archive or unarchive lots (or vessels)'
  ![How to Archive_Delete_bulk](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20Archive_Delete_bulk.png?width=670&height=112&name=How%20to%20Archive_Delete_bulk.png)
- **To find and unarchive a Fruit Lot**
  Go to the Vineyard Explorer in your left side navigation bar and search under the [Fruit Lots Explorer](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/where-to-find-your-fruit-lots?hsLang=en) tab. Be sure to use the filter to show archived lots.

You can bulk archive or unarchive Fruit lots using the Manage lots button in the Lot Explorer - be sure to use the Fruit Lot filter in the Lot picker to display Fruit Lots. ![How to archive_delete_fruit lots bulk](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20archive_delete_fruit%20lots%20bulk.png?width=670&height=174&name=How%20to%20archive_delete_fruit%20lots%20bulk.png)

### How to Delete a Vessel

- **To delete a single vessel**
  Go to the vessel detail page for that vessel and click the 'More' button in the upper right.
  A dropdown menu will appear. Within that menu, there is an option to delete the vessel.![how to archive_delete_delete vessel](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/how%20to%20archive_delete_delete%20vessel.webp?width=670&height=174&name=how%20to%20archive_delete_delete%20vessel.webp)
  Please note that if you have ever recorded an action filling this vessel, or added a vessel location, then you will not be able to delete the vessel. If so, please review the [steps above to archive it](#archive) - or, consider changing the vessel code and attributes and reusing it!![How to archive_delete_error](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20archive_delete_error.png?width=376&height=96&name=How%20to%20archive_delete_error.png)
- **To delete multiple vessels**
  You can also attempt to delete multiple vessels at once.  This is a handy tool if you made a mistake with a vessel import, for example, and need to remove multiple unused vessels at once. Use the **Manage Vessels** feature on the Vessel Explorer.
  ![How to archive_delete_bulk delete](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20archive_delete_bulk%20delete.png?width=670&height=174&name=How%20to%20archive_delete_bulk%20delete.png)Select the vessels to delete, then click the blue button on the lower right corner confirming the action.

Note that if you have ever recorded an action filling this vessel, or added a vessel location, then you will not be able to delete the vessel.  Please review the [steps above to archive it](#archive) - or, consider changing the vessel code and attributes and reusing it!

### How to Delete a Lot

Lots can only be deleted if they have no fill (i.e. are empty) and have never had any actions ever recorded on them. If a lot is created and components are entered but no actions are recorded, then the lot can be deleted.

Even if you have deleted actions on a lot, the stored history of the deleted actions will prevent deletion.

If a lot has ever had any actions recorded to it, then we recommend [archiving](https://innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/205039485-archive-lots-and-vessels?hsLang=en) the lot to remove it from your current inventory.

- **To Delete a Single Lot**
  Go to the 'More' dropdown in the top right corner of the Lot Details page > select 'Delete Lot' from the dropdown menu and confirm you want to delete that lot permanently.![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Delete_Single_Lot-png.png?width=670&height=139&name=Delete_Single_Lot-png.png)
- There is no option to bulk delete for lots.

### How to track Archived Status at a point in time

**Lots**

Use the Lot details/History tab, or the [Lot properties history report](/hc/en-us/articles/207711473-changing-lot-code-and-lot-name#history) to find a record of when a lot was archived, and who archived it.

**![Screenshot 2026-04-21 at 3.42.11 PM](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Screenshot%202026-04-21%20at%203.42.11%20PM.png?width=670&height=283&name=Screenshot%202026-04-21%20at%203.42.11%20PM.png)**

**Vessels**

There is no report that tracks or surfaces archived vessel status as of a point in time.  Here are two options to track this data:

1. Especially in the case of sold vessels, one method is to tag barrels with the date before you archive them (scanning and [tagging via InnoApp](/hc/en-us/how-to-tag?hsLang=en) is often the fastest). This option supports tracking the date, and is also an easy way to bring identified & tagged vessels from the cellar, before coming back to the desktop app and archiving them in bulk (using the tag filter on the Vessel picker).  Archived vessels will always have this tag, and you can always filter and search for it within your archived vessels.
2. Consider creating a custom task on a work order, and applying it to vessels.  These custom tasks will be recorded in the vessel history, and should also show on the winery activity feed.

Historically archived barrels are harder to demarcate. On a barrel by barrel level, the Vessel details page/Location history will record a timestamped "no location set" each time you archive/unarchive the vessel:

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Apr-21-2026-10-38-30-1148-PM.png?width=670&height=157&name=image-png-Apr-21-2026-10-38-30-1148-PM.png)
