# Depleting and Adjusting On Hand Dry Goods Inventory

This article covers:

- [Depleting an additive or packaging batch](#depleting)
- [Adjust on hand](#adjust)

#### Depleting an additive or packaging batch

Additive and packaging batches can be depleted within an Addition or Bottle action or task...

![Depleting and Adjusting On Hand Dry Goods Inventory-fully deplete](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Depleting%20and%20Adjusting%20On%20Hand%20Dry%20Goods%20Inventory-fully%20deplete.webp?width=670&height=257&name=Depleting%20and%20Adjusting%20On%20Hand%20Dry%20Goods%20Inventory-fully%20deplete.webp)

Or from the product or batch details page.

![Depleting and Adjusting On Hand Dry Goods Inventory-from batch pg](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Depleting%20and%20Adjusting%20On%20Hand%20Dry%20Goods%20Inventory-from%20batch%20pg.webp?width=670&height=158&name=Depleting%20and%20Adjusting%20On%20Hand%20Dry%20Goods%20Inventory-from%20batch%20pg.webp)

By depleting a batch, it will be removed from additive or packaging selection dropdowns in Addition and Bottle actions and tasks. If a batch is depleted by mistake, or more of the batch is received into inventory, the batch can be undepleted at any time.

Note: If the batch contains on hand amount at the time that it is depleted (eg 1kg was remaining when depleted), undepleting the batch will return the on hand amount back to inventory. To set the on hand amount to 0 (zero), we recommend using the 'Adjust on hand' option. Depleted batches must be undepleted before any changes can be made to the on hand amount.

Want to archive a batch after depleting it? Find out more [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360034896692-navigating-the-additive-explorer?hsLang=en#archiving_Batch).

#### Adjust on hand

The adjust on hand option can be used to make changes to on hand inventory - either adding or removing. Clicking the 'Adjust on hand' option in the product or batch details page will open a pop-up.

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-03-55-09-8661-PM.png?width=670&height=238&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-03-55-09-8661-PM.png)

The on hand amount is pre-populated with the current on hand amount.

Enter the corrected on hand amount and then click save to update.

Adjusting the on hand amount of a batch does not change the amount *received* of the batch. It also does not change the cost per unit of the batch.

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-03-53-17-4374-PM.png?width=670&height=263&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-03-53-17-4374-PM.png)

##### How to edit or delete a batch adjustment

To edit the date on a batch adjustment, or to delete th adjustment, click into the Batch adjustment details:

![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-Jun-24-2026-03-58-22-4301-PM.png?width=670&height=131&name=image-png-Jun-24-2026-03-58-22-4301-PM.png)

You cannot edit the amount of an adjustment, but you can delete it, and resubmit it as required.
