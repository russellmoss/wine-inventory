# Winery Activity Feed

The Winery Activity Feed displays all of the activities recorded in InnoVint, by date, across all of your inventory. You can filter for specific actions, lots code, a specific date range, and owner (if the Owner-based Permissions system is activated in your account).

![Winery Activity Feed-filter](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Winery%20Activity%20Feed-filter.webp?width=688&height=94&name=Winery%20Activity%20Feed-filter.webp)

This is a great tool if you are looking to export particular events such as Bond to Bond Transfers or Weight Adjustments, and send to a third party if they are needing this information. We export all data including reasons for volume adjustments and any notes recorded on actions.

![Winery Activity Feed-order](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Winery%20Activity%20Feed-order.webp?width=688&height=31&name=Winery%20Activity%20Feed-order.webp)

or

![Winery Activity Feed-order2](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Winery%20Activity%20Feed-order2.webp?width=688&height=31&name=Winery%20Activity%20Feed-order2.webp)

Click into any action to view more details.

Hover your mouse over a note to read it on this page without having to click into the action.

Left-facing arrows next to the action name indicate a backdated action.

![Winery Activity Feed-navigation](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Winery%20Activity%20Feed-navigation.webp?width=688&height=241&name=Winery%20Activity%20Feed-navigation.webp)
