# How to change or reset your password

This article covers:

1. [How to change your password after log-in](#change-password)
2. [What to do if you forget your password?](#forgot-pw)

**Note**: Passwords can only be changed or reset from the web interface (cellar.innovint.us). You cannot reset a password via InnoApp or SUPPLY.

InnoVint has instituted a "Zero Trust" Password policy.  InnoVint Support can no longer reset your password. Passwords can only be accessed by an individual user, and reset via a valid email address if forgotten.

### How to change your password after log-in

Once logged in, you can reset your password anytime by accessing your user account settings in the upper right-hand corner of the web interface of either MAKE or SUPPLY.

1. Click on the person icon, and then "Change password" or "Manage account":
   ![How to change or reset your password_Profile icon](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Profile%20icon.webp?width=288&height=160&name=How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Profile%20icon.webp) ![Accessing InnoVint_Reset Password_SUPPLY](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Accessing%20InnoVint_Reset%20Password_SUPPLY.png?width=327&height=160&name=Accessing%20InnoVint_Reset%20Password_SUPPLY.png)
2. In the Privacy & Security tab, click on the edit pencil to the far right of the screen:
   ![How to change or reset your password_Step 2_Edit](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Step%202_Edit.webp?width=655&height=231&name=How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Step%202_Edit.webp)
3. Enter your current password, and then enter a new password.  Click on Change Password.
   ![How to change or reset your password_Step 3](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Step%203.webp?width=655&height=306&name=How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Step%203.webp)
4. All done!

Learn more about our password guidelines [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/password-guidelines?hsLang=en).

### Forgot your password?

You can reset your password by clicking the 'Forgot password' link on the sign-in page. This is accessible from the web interface only. *Note: There is no way to reset or change your password via InnoApp or SUPPLY.*

InnoApp users should go to the InnoVint's desktop app login to reset their password via the forgot password link. **SUPPLY only** users should reach out to [InnoVint Support](mailto:support@innovint.us) for assistance.

1. On the sign-in page, click on 'Forgot password'
   ![How to change or reset your password_Forgot password_Step 1](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Forgot%20password_Step%201.webp?width=350&height=329&name=How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Forgot%20password_Step%201.webp)
2. Type in your login email address and click 'Remind me'
   ![How to change or reset your password_Forgot password_Step 2](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Forgot%20password_Step%202.webp?width=350&height=351&name=How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Forgot%20password_Step%202.webp)
3. Check your email (our reset email will come from *noreply@innovint.us*) & follow the link
4. On the Reset password page, type in and confirm your new password. Click 'Reset password'
   ![How to change or reset your password_Forgot password_Step 4](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Forgot%20password_Step%204.webp?width=350&height=361&name=How%20to%20change%20or%20reset%20your%20password_Forgot%20password_Step%204.webp)
5. All done!
