# Getting Started Checklist for MAKE ✅

### Welcome to InnoVint!

Getting your winery up and running with MAKE is a straight-forward process. If you are getting started with SUPPLY, check out that Onboarding Checklist [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/the-supply-onboarding-checklist?hsLang=en).

This article covers:

1. [First steps](#step1)

2. [Set up existing inventory](#step2)

3. [Set up additional inventory items](#step3)

4. [Additional account settings](#step4)

5. [Masterclass](#step5)

6. [TTB Compliance](#step6)

7. [Using InnoVint](#step7)

#### 1. First steps 👣

*Estimated time to complete: < 1 day*

- Open the Chrome Browser ([click here](https://www.google.com/chrome/) to download it if you don’t have it on your computer) and bookmark the link: **<https://cellar.innovint.us/>**.
- First time InnoVint users will receive an activation email.  Follow the link to verify your email and set your password. Existing users will see the new winery account available in their winery dropdown using their current login credentials. Find out more about accessing InnoVint [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/accessing-your-innovintaccount?hsLang=en).

- From Settings > Member Permissions, add [additional users](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003925952-how-to-add-or-remove-users?hsLang=en) & set their [capability levels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003925472-overview-user-permissions-and-capability-levels?hsLang=en)
- Set up your [Owners](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000877371-setting-up-your-permission-based-system?hsLang=en) via Settings > Owners (if Owner Permissions are activated)
- Access InnoVint Support Center (*Click on the question mark*  ![Getting Started Checklist_First Steps_Question Mark](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/Getting%20Started%20Checklist_First%20Steps_Question%20Mark.webp?width=30&height=20&name=Getting%20Started%20Checklist_First%20Steps_Question%20Mark.webp) *in the top right corner of InnoVint's web interface and get your answers! Search the Support Center, contact support, or go directly to InnoVint Support Center). Or, [click here.](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us?hsLang=en)*
- Download [InnoApp](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/highlights-of-the-new-mobile-experience?hsLang=en#gettingstarted) to enter data on the go 📱

#### 2. Set up your existing inventory 🌱 🍇 🍷

*Estimated time to complete: 1-7 days*

Make sure to go through these steps in order or you may have trouble recording activities, and nobody wants that!

[Step 1: Add vineyard sources](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360027033091-step-2-add-and-edit-vineyard-sources?hsLang=en)

[Step 2: Enter your vessels into the system](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/204106559-step-1-enter-your-vessels-into-the-system-with-video-?hsLang=en)

[Step 3: Enter your current wine and case good lots into the system](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204106579-step-3-enter-your-current-wine-lots-into-the-system?hsLang=en)

***Note**: If you onboard during harvest: we recommend [getting started with your incoming fruit](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360006913892-harvest-operations-flowchart?hsLang=en). Then you can onboard your previous vintages when you have time.*

[Step 4: Fill vessels with volume for each lot](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204758625-step-4-fill-vessels-with-the-volume-for-each-lot-with-video-?hsLang=en)

***Note**: Be sure to pull a physical inventory from your existing records, and decide which date you want to change over and start tracking your inventory in InnoVint.*

#### 3. Set up additional inventory items 📦 📝 🔬

*Estimated time to complete: 1-5 days*

After you get your existing wines in InnoVint, we recommend a few additional steps:

- [Enter your current dry goods](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000825066-how-to-create-additives-and-additive-batches?hsLang=en) - including additives & packaging

***Note**: Set this up prior to recording any [**Addition**](/hc/en-us/articles/204321939-how-to-record-an-addition-additive-batch-tracker-add-on-?hsLang=en) or [**Bottle**](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/207265686-how-to-record-or-edit-a-bottling-action?hsLang=en) actions to ensure you can track dry good depletions as you go.*

- Upload existing lab data using the [Analysis Import](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115002687291-how-to-import-analyses-via-csv?hsLang=en) action
- Add any other lot, vessel, dry good, or vineyard information by uploading a pdf, photo or file to any [Note](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360009672091-how-to-add-a-photo-note?hsLang=en) on a details page
- Create and add [Tags](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204503439-what-are-tags-used-for-in-innovint-?hsLang=en) to your inventory items and vineyards to further track or sort them
- Print your [QR code vessel labels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000609563-how-do-i-generate-qr-code-vessel-labels-to-print-?hsLang=en) and [lot labels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360002989051-how-do-i-generate-lot-labels-to-print-?hsLang=en)
- Add [vessel locations](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vessel-location-tracking?hsLang=en) to your vessels

#### 4. Additional account settings  🗂

*Estimated time to complete: 1-3 days*

Personalize your account even more by setting up these additional account settings based on your needs:

- Set up your [dip charts](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360050058652-managing-and-using-dip-charts-in-innovint?hsLang=en)
- Select your [harvest settings](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115001094151-harvest-settings-receive-fruit-options-and-expected-yield-?hsLang=en) - explore receive fruit options and set expected yields
- Create your [analysis panels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204504149-analysis-panels-how-to-create-save-and-delete?hsLang=en) for quick access
- Create your most frequently used [work orders as templates](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/205941995-how-to-create-and-use-work-order-templates?hsLang=en) & set your default [work order submission](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360050001811-using-work-orders-in-innovint?hsLang=en#workordersubmission) preference
- Enter your frequently used [Shipping Locations](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/locations?hsLang=en) for [Bills of Lading](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360055473151-how-to-create-a-bill-of-lading-bol-?hsLang=en) and [bond to bond transfers](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360018542692-bond-to-bond-transfers?hsLang=en)
- Add any additional [bonds](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/how-to-add-a-new-bond?hsLang=en) to your account by contacting us at [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us)
- [TankNET Integration](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/overview-tanknet-integration?hsLang=en) Set up (with TankNET add-on activated)
- Set up your [ETS Integration](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115001540266-ets-integration-overview?hsLang=en)

- Set up your [Tank Maps](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/tank-maps?hsLang=en)!

#### 5. Masterclass 🏅

*Estimated time to complete: 1-3 days*

All your inventory is in and your settings are ready for use. Utilize these tools to get the most out of your InnoVint account:

- Enter your [starting costs](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003479571-getting-started-with-the-cost-tracking-feature?hsLang=en) as of a set point in time (if you have the FINANCE module and COGS Tracking is activated)
  - Then, begin entering your [indirect costs](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/allocate-costs-indirect-direct?hsLang=en#costitem), such as overheads, on a regular basis
  - Additionally, set up your [direct costs](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/allocate-costs-indirect-direct?hsLang=en#directcosts), such as fruit and packaging that will be allocated via actions in InnoVint
- Enter your vineyard data and contracts:
  - Historical and current vineyard [events and analyses](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360026806092-how-to-record-and-track-data-and-analyses-on-vineyard-blocks?hsLang=en) (if GROW is activated)
  - Current vineyard [contracts](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360027080951-how-to-manage-vineyard-contracts?hsLang=en) (available at specific subscription levels)
- Use [Custom actions or Custom tasks](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204848455-using-a-custom-action-or-custom-task?hsLang=en) to personalize your workflow and apply tasks to the entire winery
- Create [analysis reports](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360052048151-custom-analysis-reports?hsLang=en) or set up your own [Custom Reports](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/custom-reports?hsLang=en) to view your data the way you like it
- Use the [Winery Activity Feed](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000258166-winery-activity-feed?hsLang=en) to view all activities recorded in InnoVint
- View your additive & packaging histories using the [Additive & Packaging History Reports](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000871603-reporting-on-additions-per-additive-or-batch-number?hsLang=en#report)
- Learn more about how to track your [case goods inventory](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/case-goods-feature-introduction-and-overview?hsLang=en)
- Learn about using the [sparkling workflow](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/sparkling-wine-production-feature-overview?hsLang=en) in InnoVint 🍾 (if Sparkling activated)

#### 6. TTB Compliance 🗃

*Estimated time to complete: 1 day*

When the time comes to pull your government reports, know the tips and tricks to get that data fast.

- [Add](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003925952-how-to-add-or-remove-users?hsLang=en) your compliance specialist as a user and set their [capability level](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003925472-overview-user-permissions-and-capability-levels?hsLang=en) to *Read Only* (if you only want them to only be able to pull reports)
- Need to report your volume in tax classes other than <16% or 16-21%? Utilize the [State Compliance Report by Bond](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/what-is-the-state-compliance-by-bond-report?hsLang=en) (looking at you California wineries 👀).
- Use the [Tax class declaration report](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/207936576-declare-or-edit-tax-class?hsLang=en#report) to declare all of your wines in one go at the end of harvest!
- Run the [TTB 5120.17 report](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/209726993-generate-and-download-the-ttb-5120-17-report?hsLang=en) and use the [TTB Audit Report](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/understanding-the-ttb-audit-report?hsLang=en) to deep dive into your action history
- Utilize the [lock backdating](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360020396351-winery-activity-lock-backdating?hsLang=en) option to ensure no changes will be made to previously submitted reports 🔒

#### 7. Start using InnoVint! 🛫

*Estimated time to complete entire onboarding checklist: 1-3 weeks*

Now you're ready to start using InnoVint *daily* to enter analysis, record actions and work orders, communicate with your team and generate valuable reports.

Depending on your winery workflow, there are a couple of options to enter data:

1. Enter actions directly via Direct actions (great for *small* teams & quick data entry)
   - Use the *Record action* button from the global bar or from any lot details page
2. Schedule out your work and assign it to your team using [Work Orders](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360050001811-using-work-orders-in-innovint?hsLang=en) (great for *larger* teams or planning work)
   - Access *Create work order* from the global bar or through the **Work Order Explorer** - leverage [work order templates](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/205941995-how-to-create-and-use-work-order-templates?hsLang=en) for your standard protocols!
3. Or, use a combination of both!

If you have any questions, please [submit a ticket](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/kb-tickets/new?hsLang=en) or email InnoVint Customer Success at [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us). We're here to help you get the most out of InnoVint!
