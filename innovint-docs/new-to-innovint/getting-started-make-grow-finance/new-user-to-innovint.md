We are excited to welcome you to InnoVint and want to give you some resources to be able to dive straight into your winery account. We'll guide you through below. Are you a new user to SUPPLY? Get resources for SUPPLY [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/supply?hsLang=en).

#### 1. First steps 👣

- Open the Chrome Browser ([click here](https://www.google.com/chrome/) to download it if you don’t have it on your computer) and bookmark the link: **<https://cellar.innovint.us/>**.
- Brand new InnoVint users will receive an activation email to validate your email address and set your password.  Next, login with your email and password. Get more information on accessing InnoVint [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/accessing-your-innovintaccount?hsLang=en).
- Access InnoVint Support Center. Click on the question mark  ![Getting Started Checklist_First Steps_Question Mark](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/Getting%20Started%20Checklist_First%20Steps_Question%20Mark.webp?width=30&height=20&name=Getting%20Started%20Checklist_First%20Steps_Question%20Mark.webp) in the top right corner of InnoVint's desktop platform and get your answers! Search the Support Center, contact support, or go directly to InnoVint Support Center. Or, just [click here.](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us?hsLang=en)
- Download our mobile app, [InnoApp](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/highlights-of-the-new-mobile-experience?hsLang=en#gettingstarted), to enter data on the go 📱
- If you have access to more than one account, switch between accounts using the top left winery toggle.
- If you are a client at a custom crush facility (with Owner Permissions activated), please reach out to your host winery for specifics about how to engage with InnoVint.

#### 2. View your existing inventory 🌱 🍇 🍷

Utilize the left hand navigation to move between modules within InnoVint.

- **Lot Explorer**: View and manage current juice and wine lots
  - Learn how to [add new lots](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204106579-step-3-enter-your-current-wine-lots-into-the-system?hsLang=en) and fill them with volume via a [B2B Transfer In](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360018542692-bond-to-bond-transfers?hsLang=en)
- **Vessel Explorer**: View and manage vessels
  - [Enter new vessels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/204106559-step-1-enter-your-vessels-into-the-system-with-video-?hsLang=en) or [edit current vessels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/206240653-change-or-edit-vessel-details?hsLang=en)
- **[Case Good Explorer](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/case-goods-feature-introduction-and-overview?hsLang=en)**: View & manage current case good lots (if Case Goods is activated)
- **[Dry Good Explorer](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360034896692-navigating-the-additive-explorer?hsLang=en)**: View and manage your additives and packaging supplies
  - [Receive more dry goods inventory](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000825066-how-to-create-additives-and-additive-batches?hsLang=en)
- **Vineyard Explorer**: View and manage vineyards and blocks
  - [Learn how to add or edit vineyard and block sources](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360027033091-step-2-add-and-edit-vineyard-sources?hsLang=en)
  - Users with GROW activated can [track vineyard events and analysis](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360026806092-how-to-record-and-track-data-and-analyses-on-vineyard-blocks?hsLang=en)
- [**Report Explorer**](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/report-explorer?hsLang=en): View activity, custom, multi-winery, compliance, and analysis reports
  - Use the [Winery Activity Feed](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000258166-winery-activity-feed?hsLang=en) to view all recorded activities
  - [Analysis Reporting](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/205001715-analysis-reporting?hsLang=en)
  - Understand which [exports](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/213128166-what-exports-are-available-in-innovint-vessel_expl?hsLang=en) are available

- **[COGS Tracking Explorer](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003095311-feature-overview-cost-allocation-specifics?hsLang=en)**: View and add costs to the system (if you have FINANCE and COGS Tracking activated)
- **Settings**: Manage your preferences and settings
  - If you are an *Admin* understand your [capabilities](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003925472-overview-user-permissions-and-capability-levels?hsLang=en) (including how to [add additional users](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003925952-how-to-add-or-remove-users?hsLang=en))

#### 3. Learn how to record activity ✅ 📝

Depending on your winery workflow, there are a couple of options to enter data:

1. Enter actions directly via [Direct actions](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/using-innovint?hsLang=en#recording-actions) (great for *small* teams & quick data entry)
   - Use the *Record action* button from the global bar or from any lot details page
2. Schedule out your work and assign it to your team using [Work Orders](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360050001811-using-work-orders-in-innovint?hsLang=en) (great for *large* teams)
   - Access *Create work order* from the global bar or through the **Work Order Explorer**

Or, use a combination of both!

#### 4. Additional account items that may need to be set up 📦 📝 🔬

Personalize your account even more by setting up these additional account settings based on your needs:

- Set up your [dip charts](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360050058652-managing-and-using-dip-charts-in-innovint?hsLang=en)
- Print your [QR code vessel labels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000609563-how-do-i-generate-qr-code-vessel-labels-to-print-?hsLang=en) and [lot labels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360002989051-how-do-i-generate-lot-labels-to-print-?hsLang=en)
- Select your [harvest settings](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115001094151-harvest-settings-receive-fruit-options-and-expected-yield-?hsLang=en) - receive fruit options and expected yield (admin only)
- Create your [analysis panels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204504149-analysis-panels-how-to-create-save-and-delete?hsLang=en) for quick access
- Create your most frequently used [work orders as templates](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/205941995-how-to-create-and-use-work-order-templates?hsLang=en) & set your default [work order submission](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360050001811-using-work-orders-in-innovint?hsLang=en#workordersubmission) preference
- Save frequently used shipping [locations](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/locations?hsLang=en) for [Bills of Lading](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360055473151-how-to-create-a-bill-of-lading-bol-?hsLang=en) & [bond to bond transfers](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360018542692-bond-to-bond-transfers?hsLang=en)
- Add [vessel locations](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vessel-location-tracking?hsLang=en) to your vessels

#### 5. Masterclass 🏅

Utilize these tools to get the most out of your InnoVint account:

- Use a [Custom action or Custom task](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204848455-using-a-custom-action-or-custom-task?hsLang=en) to personalize your workflow and apply tasks to the entire winery
- Utilize bulk manage buttons to quickly make changes to lot and vessel inventory
  - Such as [archiving/unarchiving](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/205039485-archive-lots-and-vessels?hsLang=en), [tag addition/removal](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204503449-adding-editing-or-removing-tags?hsLang=en), [lot stages](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204339859-how-to-change-lot-stage?hsLang=en#bulk), and [vessel details](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/206240653-change-or-edit-vessel-details?hsLang=en#bulk)
- Learn how to upload existing lab data using the [Analysis Import](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115002687291-how-to-import-analyses-via-csv?hsLang=en) action
- Add any other lot, vessel, dry good, or vineyard information by uploading a pdf, photo or file to any [Note](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360009672091-how-to-add-a-photo-note?hsLang=en) on a details page 📝
- Create and add [Tags](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/204503439-what-are-tags-used-for-in-innovint-?hsLang=en) to your inventory items and vineyards to further track or sort them
- Save time be using imports to quickly upload [lots](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/how-to-import-lots-via-csv?hsLang=en), [vessels](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/how-to-import-vessels-via-csv-file?hsLang=en), [dry goods](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/how-to-import-dry-goods?hsLang=en), and [vineyard events](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/how-to-import-vineyard-events-via-csv-file?hsLang=en)
- Create [your own analysis reports](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360052048151-custom-analysis-reports?hsLang=en) to view your data the way you like it
- Customize and save your own reports by using [Custom Reports](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/custom-reports?hsLang=en)
- View your additive or packaging history using [our dry good history reports](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000871603-reporting-on-additions-per-additive-or-batch-number?hsLang=en#report)
- Enter any new winery costs (if the Finance module and [COGS Tracking](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/cogs-tracking-in-innovint?hsLang=en) is activated) 💰
  - Enter your indirect cost items: [dry goods](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003467391-Assigning-costs-to-additive-batches?hsLang=en) and [fruit costs](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003336831-Assigning-fruit-costs-to-vineyard-blocks?hsLang=en)
  - Enter your [direct cost items](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360003203451-Adding-a-cost-item-indirect-as-an-overhead-cost-?hsLang=en), such as invoices, overhead, and other costs

- Enter your vineyard data and contracts:
  - Historical and current vineyard [events and analyses](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360026806092-how-to-record-and-track-data-and-analyses-on-vineyard-blocks?hsLang=en)
  - Current and upcoming vineyard [contracts](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360027080951-how-to-manage-vineyard-contracts?hsLang=en) (if Finance and Grow are both activated)
- Learn about using the [sparkling workflow](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/sparkling-wine-production-feature-overview?hsLang=en) in InnoVint 🍾 (if Sparkling is activated)
- Learn more about how to track your [case goods inventory](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/case-goods-feature-introduction-and-overview?hsLang=en) (if Case Goods is activated)
- Learn more about [owners](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/218236223-owner-based-permissions-and-member-capabilities-overview-article-?hsLang=en) (if Owner Permissions are activated)

#### 6. TTB Compliance 🗃

When the time comes to pull your government reports, know the tips and tricks to get that data fast.

- Need to report your volume in tax classes other than <16% or 16-21%? Utilize the Inventory at Point in Time Report or the [State Compliance by Bond](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/what-is-the-state-compliance-by-bond-report?hsLang=en) report to generate this information (looking at you California wineries 👀).
- Use the [Tax class declaration report](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/207936576-declare-or-edit-tax-class?hsLang=en#report) to declare all of your wines in one go today!
- Run the [TTB 5120.17 report](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/209726993-generate-and-download-the-ttb-5120-17-report?hsLang=en) and use the [TTB Audit Report](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/understanding-the-ttb-audit-report?hsLang=en) to deep dive into your action history
- Utilize the [lock backdating](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360020396351-winery-activity-lock-backdating?hsLang=en) option to ensure no changes will be made to previously submitted reports 🔒
