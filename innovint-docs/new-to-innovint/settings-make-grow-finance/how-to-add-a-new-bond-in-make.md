# How to Add a New Bond in MAKE

Need to add a new bond to your InnoVint MAKE account? Simply submit a ticket from the Support Center (link in the top right corner), *Subject: Add new bond*.

All we need to add the new bond is:

- Bond Registry Number
- DBA name
- Legal name, if different
- Optional to populate the TTB report:
  - Address
  - Phone
  - EIN

Clients get an unlimited number of bonds in their account at no additional cost.

#### Bonds & Owners

If you have our [Owner-Based Permission System,](/hc/en-us/articles/218236223-owner-based-permissions-and-member-capabilities-overview-article-?hsLang=en) don't forget to associate your new bond with an Owner if necessary.

If this owner already has a bond number associated with it and it needs to be updated, contact InnoVint Support at [support@innovint.us](mailto:support@innovint.us).

If an owner you just added does not have the new bond shown next to their name under the “owners” tab, try refreshing your browser tab first.  Reach out to InnoVint support if the bond number does not appear.
