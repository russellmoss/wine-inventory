# Tracking Mead in InnoVint

This article covers how to add a honey source in InnoVint to track mead production.

1. First, the "Agriculture" source option must be activated for your facility. Contact support@innovint.us if you need the "Agriculture" source option enabled.
2. Click into the Vineyard Explorer from the side bar menu, and in the upper right hand corner click "Add Vineyard."
3. Add a new "vineyard" for the honey. Be mindful that the appellation source *must* match the varietal source (notice in the image below how there is the small "Agriculture" subtitle).
   ![Tracking Mead in InnoVint-block ag](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Tracking%20Mead%20in%20InnoVint-block%20ag.webp?width=549&height=421&name=Tracking%20Mead%20in%20InnoVint-block%20ag.webp)

4. After your block is added you can create the lot the same way as you would with wine. [Receive your honey in weight,](/hc/en-us/articles/360005125552-receive-fruit?hsLang=en) as a "fruit lot", and then [process it to volume](/hc/en-us/articles/360006828911-process-fruit-to-volume?hsLang=en), and add water to bring to your fermenting volume.

For more information on setting up your InnoVint account, refer to the [Getting Started Checklist](/hc/en-us/innovint-onboarding-checklist?hsLang=en).
