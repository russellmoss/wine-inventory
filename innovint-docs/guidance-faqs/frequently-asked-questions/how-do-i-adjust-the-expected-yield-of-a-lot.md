# How do I adjust the Expected Yield of a lot?

There are 4 ways to adjust the Expected Yield of a lot in InnoVint:

1. [In Harvest Settings](https://innovint-6865708.hs-sites.com/hc/en-us/articles/115001094151-harvest-settings-receive-fruit-options-and-expected-yield-?hsLang=en#settingexpyield) - adjust Expected Yield per variety
2. [At lot creation](/hc/en-us/articles/360006478872-process-fruit-to-tons#process-to-lot) - set the Expected Yield for a single juice lot when processing fruit to tons
3. Via [Juice bleed/Saignée](/hc/en-us/articles/204651979-juice-bleed-saign%C3%A9e#expectedyield)- an automatic adjustment is calculated after action is recorded
4. In [the Lot Details page](/hc/en-us/articles/205001375-what-can-i-see-on-a-lot-details-page-#dashboard) - click on the blue pencil to adjust the expected yield of a lot
