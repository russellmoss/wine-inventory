# What is the difference between a Fruit Lot and a Juice/Wine Lot?

If you are onboarding your lot inventory into InnoVint, you may notice that there are different types of lots to choose from: Juice/Wine lot and Fruit lot. Each type of lot has a specific purpose in InnoVint.

![What is the difference between a Fruit Lot and a Juice Wine Lot-fruit vs wine lot](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/What%20is%20the%20difference%20between%20a%20Fruit%20Lot%20and%20a%20Juice%20Wine%20Lot-fruit%20vs%20wine%20lot.webp?width=188&height=201&name=What%20is%20the%20difference%20between%20a%20Fruit%20Lot%20and%20a%20Juice%20Wine%20Lot-fruit%20vs%20wine%20lot.webp)

### Fruit Lots

- - Fruit lots are vintage-specific lots to help you track your fruit from the vineyard to the crush pad.
  - If you do not have GROW (formerly known as Advanced Vineyard Tracking)  activated, Fruit lots are the only way to [track vineyard data](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/212998763-how-to-track-vineyard-data-in-fruit-lots?hsLang=en) in InnoVint. If you are measuring leaf water potential, brix, acid, phenolics, or any other analytics in the vineyard you will want to record them on the fruit lot to track maturity and for reference in the future.
  - Fruit lots are the antecedents to your Juice/Wine lots and are how InnoVint ties compositional data between your received and processed fruit. They are also the only type of lot that are tracked solely in weight. Find out how to create Fruit lots [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/360005034292-how-to-create-a-fruit-lot-in-innovint?hsLang=en).
  - If you have the Owner-based permissions system activated in InnoVint, you can also track multiple fruit lots separately for different owners that source fruit from the same vineyard and block.
  - You will only utilize Fruit lots in Receive Fruit (to fill) or Process Fruit actions or tasks (to deplete).  As your fruit is processed (via the Process Fruit to Volume/Weight actions or tasks), a Juice/Wine lot will be created, moving your fruit tonnage into the new Juice/Wine lot tracked in volume or weight.
  - Find your Fruit Lot Explorer next to the Vineyard Explorer

### Juice/Wine Lots

- - Juice/Wine lots track movements and data from the crush pad through to bottling.
  - Juice/Wine lots are created when:
    - A Fruit lot is processed to gallons or tons
    - You onboard current on-hand inventory
    - A facility receives bulk wine, juice, or concentrate
  - Find your Juice/Wine lots in the Lot Explorer.
