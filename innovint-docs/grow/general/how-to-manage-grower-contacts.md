# How to manage grower contacts

You can record and reference a grower on any vineyard in InnoVint.  With InnoVint's GROW product activated, you can also store and review important grower information within the platform.

The article covers:

- [How to add or change a grower on a vineyard](#addchangevineyard)
- [The Grower Explorer](#growerexplorer)
  - [How to add or edit grower details](#addedit)

### How to add or change a grower on a vineyard

Go to the Vineyard Explorer and select a vineyard from the list. Go the Vineyard Details page.

Click on the blue pencil in the top right corner of the Details card in the Vineyard Details Dashboard.

![How to manage grower contacts-edit vineyard attributes](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-edit%20vineyard%20attributes.webp?width=399&height=374&name=How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-edit%20vineyard%20attributes.webp)

Select a new grower from the dropdown or add a new grower by clicking ![How to manage grower contacts-add grower button](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-add%20grower%20button.webp?width=109&height=25&name=How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-add%20grower%20button.webp).

![How to manage grower contacts-dropdown](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-dropdown.webp?width=539&height=350&name=How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-dropdown.webp)

### Grower Explorer

Users with GROW activated will have additional options to view and manage Growers.

To begin, click on  ![](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Knowledge%20Base%20Import/support.innovint.ushcarticle_attachments360026565752Vineyards_icon_text__1_-Feb-26-2021-10-19-27-82-PM.png?width=94&height=50&name=support.innovint.ushcarticle_attachments360026565752Vineyards_icon_text__1_-Feb-26-2021-10-19-27-82-PM.png) in the left navigation bar. Then go to the Grower Explorer either via the slide over Vineyard menu, or via the Vineyard Dashboard.

![How to manage grower contacts-dashboard](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-dashboard.webp?width=688&height=59&name=How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-dashboard.webp)

The Grower Explorer lists all the Growers currently in your account. You can easily view the company name, point of contact with phone number and grower address. It will also show you if a grower is designated as an Estate grower and how many total vineyards are managed by that grower.

![How to manage grower contacts-grower explorer](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-grower%20explorer.webp?width=688&height=156&name=How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-grower%20explorer.webp)

### How to add or edit grower details

Users with GROW activated will have additional options to add/edit growers via the Grower Explorer

#### To add a grower

Click on ![How to manage grower contacts-add button](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-add%20button.webp?width=109&height=25&name=How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-add%20button.webp) in the top right corner of the Grower Explorer. Alternatively, you can add a new grower when creating a new vineyard.

Enter the information and any details for the grower. Select *Estate* if this is an estate grower.

![How to manage grower contacts-add info](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-add%20info.webp?width=538&height=579&name=How%20to%20manage%20grower%20contacts-add%20info.webp)

#### **To edit grower information**

Select the grower from the list in the Grower Explorer. Update the fields in the popup, and click *Update Grower*.
