# 2022 Harvest Wrap

We've made it through another harvest! In this video, we walk you though many tips and tricks to finish up harvest in your InnoVint account (and prep for year-end). This session covers:

- Bulk updating Lot Stages *(3:10)*
- Bulk updating Tax Classes *(5:30)*
- Post-harvest inventory clean up:
  - Adjusting on-hand amounts of additives in the Dry Goods Explorer *(9:34)*
  - Archiving any lingering Fruit Lots in the Fruit Lot Explorer *(11:23)*
  - Removing Case Goods lots as Taxpaid in the Case Goods Explorer *(14:32)*
- Setting up Vessel Location Tracking *(18:41)*
