# Prepare Your Winery for Harvest

In this month's Academy session we dive into some tricks and tips to prep your production space for the upcoming harvest season.

This video covers:

- Entering in new and removing old users
- Vessel locations and printing vessel QR codes
- Setting lot stages, cleaning up your Lot Explorer, and getting rid of old fruit lots
- Adding in your new barrels quickly and easily
- Making sure you have enough harvest additives

and so much more!
