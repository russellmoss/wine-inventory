# Spring Clean your Winery for Harvest

In this video, we help you get organized for harvest with a winery spring cleaning. This session covers:

- Reviewing Team InnoVint's [Harvest Prep Checklist](#Harvest-Prep-Checklist) *(3:03)*
- Leveraging work orders to keep track of all those to-dos before harvest *(5:00)*
- Tidying up your account settings *(9:29)*
- Tricks to update and clear out tags *(14:55)*
- Getting inventory up-to-date:
  - Vessels *(21:00)*
  - Vineyards *(29:55)*
  - Dry Goods *(31:24)*

### Harvest Prep Checklist

- Scheduling/performing equipment maintenance
- Inventorying dry goods and ordering fermentation supplies
- Repainting barrel row lanes
- Prep/pressure wash picking bins
- Hydrating/gassing empty barrels
- Checking eye wash stations and safety stations
- Reorganizing fittings board
- Receiving and stickering barrels
- Writing/updating intern SOPs
- Establishing work schedules (PT and FT)
- Forecasting rough harvest dates
- Forecasting estimated % over/under yield-wise for purchasing decisions
- Securing any final grape or juice contracts
- Posting help-wanted ads
- Posting equipment needed ads
- Selling used barrels not needed for harvest
- Buying beer to get everyone through harvest
- Scheduling harvest lunches
- Stocking the kitchen with sugar and energy drinks
- Posting memes on [The Punchdown](https://punchdown.innovint.us/home) to boost industry morale
