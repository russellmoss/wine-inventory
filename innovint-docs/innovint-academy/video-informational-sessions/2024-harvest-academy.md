# 2024 Harvest Academy

Let us help you get harvest-ready with our latest-and-greatest tips (and new functionality!) to streamline your 2024 season!  This session covers:

- Scheduling and receiving fruit *(3:36)*
- Processing fruit and transferring volume to weight *(12:24)*
- Tracking your fermentations and multi-lot Drain and Press workflows *(21:12)*
  - *UPDATE! As of July 2025, you can disregard the multi-lot drain & press workflows and instead use the updated [Drain and Press action](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/205552639-drain-and-press?hsLang=en) for multiple lots!*
- Using Custom Reports for harvest *(34:20)*
