# Pre-Bottling

In this episode of InnoVint Academy we will cover tips and workflows for Pre-Bottling activity in InnoVint. Planning for bottling can be a pain, but we are here to make it easy and efficient for you. In this episode we will cover:

- How our actions and integrations can help you prepare for bottling day
- Dip charts and how they can be easily accessed in our mobile app
- Blending and Analysis
- Fining, Stabilization, and Temperature Management
- Using work orders for labor and utility planning

Happy Bottling!
