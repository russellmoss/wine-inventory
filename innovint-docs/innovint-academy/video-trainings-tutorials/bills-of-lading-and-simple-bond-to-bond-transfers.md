# Bills of Lading and Simple Bond to Bond Transfers

In this month's Academy session we dive into Bills of Lading and how to complete simple Bond to Bond Transfers. Simple B2B transfers are used to move wine into and out of your bond, to or from a non-affiliated or non-InnoVint location. This action adjusts shipped gallons in your inventory and updates the on-hand volumes reported on the 5120.17 TTB report.

This video covers:

- How to create and use shipping locations
- Complete Bond to Bond (B2B) Transfers in direct actions
- How to generate your Bills of Lading (BOL)
- How to generate a BOL outside of B2B actions
