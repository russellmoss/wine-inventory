# MAKE: Case Goods Academy

In this month's Academy session we dive into Case Goods Management. This is a great way to track your lots after they have been bottled. In this module you can also add packaging and track costs associated with your finished lots.

This video covers:

- Bottling into Case Goods lots
- Other ways to add Case Goods Inventory
- Review of the Case Goods lot details page
- Recording actions on Case Goods lots
- Taxpaid Report and Compliance
