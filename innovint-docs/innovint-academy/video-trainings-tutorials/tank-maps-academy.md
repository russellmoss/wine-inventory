# Tank Maps Academy

In this session, our CTO takes you through an in-depth training of our Tank Maps feature. This session covers:

- A general overview of the Tank Maps feature *(1:18)*
- Creating your maps *(9:48)*
- Pro-tips on maximizing your use of the feature
  - Custom-position your view of Tank Maps in 3D mode *(5:47)*
  - Multi-select and move vessels during editing *(11:52)*
  - Multi-select and rotate vessels during editing *(14:29)*
  - Use "Add Vessels" dropdown list to locate vessels on the map *(16:17)*
- Computer requirements *(19:12)*
- The future of Tank Maps *(20:19)*
