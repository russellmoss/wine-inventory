# Dry Goods: Additive & Packaging Tracking

In this Academy session we review the Packaging and Additive features in InnoVint via the Dry Goods Module. We cover:

- receiving new or additional batches
- editing previously received dry goods
- adding packaging within an action
- reporting
- costing in Dry Goods
- custom crush permissions in Dry Goods

Want to see it in writing? Learn more about how to create and receive dry goods [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/articles/115000825066-how-to-create-additives-and-additive-batches?hsLang=en).
