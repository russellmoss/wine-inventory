# InnoVint + Barrelwise Integration

#### This article covers:

[What is it?](#what)
[How it works](#how)
[Setting up the integration](#who)

#### What is it?

Through this integration, you can synchronize up-to-date barrel and lot information from InnoVint with real-time free SO₂ data from the BarrelWise FS1 System.

For customers of both InnoVint and [Barrelwise](https://www.barrelwise.ca/), you can complete free SO₂ analysis and SO₂ additions in the cellar via the Barrelwise system with this data immediately available to your winemaking team and the lab. Analysis results are uploaded into InnoVint alongside all your other critical winemaking data.

#### InnoVint + Barrelwise Integration-bbl wise How it works

Your InnoVint lot and vessel codes populate the Barrelwise system automatically, and then your team does its thing checking barrels in the cellar.

Barrelwise pushes free SO2 lot composite analysis data (your Barrelwise virtual composite result) into InnoVint.  Use Barrelwise's addition records to quickly enter Addition actions or update tasks in InnoVint.

#### Setting up the integration

Reach out to Barrelwise directly at [hello@barrelwise.ca](mailto:hello@barrelwise.ca). to check your subscription and get started! Access to the integration will be available based on your Barrelwise subscription.
