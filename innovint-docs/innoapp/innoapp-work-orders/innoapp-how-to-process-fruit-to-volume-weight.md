# InnoApp: How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight

This article explains how to complete and submit Process Fruit tasks in InnoApp:

- [Process Fruit to Volume](#volume)
- [Process Fruit to Weight](#weight)

#### Process Fruit to Volume

1. Select your work order and tap into the Task details
   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-1](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-1.webp?width=173&height=319&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-1.webp)
2. From the Task details page, you will be able to view Fruit lot details, but not edit the lot or processed weight.
   ▪️**In the case where you have requested to leave a specific amount remaining:**
   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-2 spec amount](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20spec%20amount.webp?width=512&height=132&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20spec%20amount.webp)
   you will see the task specify the amount to leave, as well as the amount to remove from the fruit lot:
   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-2 process](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20process.webp?width=186&height=346&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20process.webp)

   ▪️**In the case where you have requested to leave a specific amount to process**:

   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-2 spec process](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20spec%20process.webp?width=476&height=128&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20spec%20process.webp)
   you will see the task specify the amount to process, as well as the amount to leave in the fruit lot:
   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-2 process leave in](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20process%20leave%20in.webp?width=186&height=344&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-2%20process%20leave%20in.webp)
3. You can view, but not edit, your process steps.
4. Tap into the "To" lot to choose or add vessels, and to specify the ending fill(s) in the available field(s). The vessel fill and check off function is the same for other movement tasks:
   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-4 to](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-4%20to.webp?width=171&height=304&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-4%20to.webp)  ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-4 edit vol](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-4%20edit%20vol.webp?width=336&height=312&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-4%20edit%20vol.webp)
5. After updating all lot and vessel volumes, tap the back arrow to return to the Task details screen and **Complete** the task.
   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-5](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-5.webp?width=170&height=319&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-5.webp)
6. If permissions are available, and the task is Complete, you will be able to Submit the task as of the desired time:
   ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-5-1](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-5-1.webp?width=163&height=306&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-5-1.webp)  ![InnoApp How to Process Fruit to Volume & Weight-6.b](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-6.b.webp?width=329&height=193&name=InnoApp%20How%20to%20Process%20Fruit%20to%20Volume%20%26%20Weight-6.b.webp)

#### FAQ

**Q. I don't see open fields to update the volume or weight - how do I update the volume or weight in a vessel?**

*A. Make sure you have clicked into the Lot details screen.  If you do not have any white highlighted fields to update, please check to see if the task is already completed.  You will not be able to update volumes after the task is completed.*

**Q. How can I unstart or uncomplete a task?**

*A. Use the "**More**" menu, found on the **Task details** screen. You will be able to SKIP a task if it is unstarted, to UNSTART a task if it is started, or to UNCOMPLETE a task, if it is completed. You will not see the **More** menu here after task submission.*

**Q. Can I add another lot to the task?**

*A. InnoApp does not support adding additional lots at this time. You may be able to add more "**To**" lots by editing the work order in the desktop app.*
