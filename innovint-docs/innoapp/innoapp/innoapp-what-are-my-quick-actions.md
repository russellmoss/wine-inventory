# InnoApp: What are my Quick Actions?

This article provides a summary of the following Quick Action functions on InnoVint's InnoApp homescreen. Get straight into data entry on your mobile device without any additional clicks:

- [Scan QR Code](#QR)
- [Tag Vessels](#Tag)
- [Update Vessel Locations](#locations)
- [Photo Note](#photo)
- [Record Action](#actions)
- [Record Analysis](#Analysis)
- [Record Brix (Sugar) & Temp](#sugar)
- [Record Vineyard Event](#events)
- [Crop Estimate](#estimates)

**![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-menu](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-menu.webp?width=250&height=330&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-menu.webp)**

Don't forget! All this data saves while you are off-line and out of range!

#### Scan QR Code

Jump straight into the scanning screen so you can scan a vessel QR code and find out what's inside! The QR code will take you straight to the [vessel's details page](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vessel-explorer-page?hsLang=en#Details).

#### Tag Vessels

Use this Quick Action to scan vessel QR Codes, and add, remove, and update Tags on multiple vessels almost instantly. Find more details [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/how-to-tag?hsLang=en#quick).

![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-tag vessels](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-tag%20vessels.webp?width=256&height=385&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-tag%20vessels.webp)

#### Update Vessel Locations

Use your phone and vessel QR Codes to quickly select and update one or more vessel locations (but not positions) with just a few "clicks". Find more details on how to update locations on InnoApp [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/mobile-app-how-to-update-vessel-location?hsLang=en).  Read an overview on the Vessel Location Tracking module [here.](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vessel-location-tracking?hsLang=en)

![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-update vessel locations](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-update%20vessel%20locations.webp?width=250&height=189&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-update%20vessel%20locations.webp)

#### Photo Note

Fun fact: this doesn't exist yet! We got excited, but for now, you'll have to access photo notes via the note functions that exist in the [Lots](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/lots?hsLang=en#actions), [Vessels,](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vessel-explorer-page?hsLang=en#Details) [Vineyard](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vineyard-mobile-app?hsLang=en#actions) and [Block](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vineyard-mobile-app?hsLang=en#block) screens.

![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-photo note](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-photo%20note.webp?width=255&height=125&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-photo%20note.webp)

#### Record Action

A mighty tool for harvest: record pumpovers, punchdowns, stirring, dry icing, delestage and pulsair actions right from your phone. You can even enter any kind of custom action you want on a Lot from your mobile app.  You can also record these actions from the Lot, or Vessel Details pages.

![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-record pop action](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20pop%20action.webp?width=268&height=325&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20pop%20action.webp)   ![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-record custom action](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20custom%20action.webp?width=262&height=328&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20custom%20action.webp)

#### Record Analysis

Record [Lot](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/mobile-app-how-to-record-analysis?hsLang=en#lot) or [Vineyard Block](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/mobile-app-how-to-record-analysis?hsLang=en#vineyard_analysis) Analyses quickly.

![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-record analysis](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20analysis.webp?width=257&height=134&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20analysis.webp)

Use any existing panels in your account, or add ad hoc results as needed, wherever you are.

![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-record lot comp](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20lot%20comp.webp?width=259&height=265&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20lot%20comp.webp)   ![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-record vineyard block](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20vineyard%20block.webp?width=268&height=259&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-record%20vineyard%20block.webp)

#### Record Sugars/Temps for Harvest

If you are using printed Fermentation worksheets, use this [quick link](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/mobile-app-how-to-record-analysis?hsLang=en#Bs_Ts) to record your Brix (or Baumé) and temps.

![InnoApp What are my Quick Actions-brix temp](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-brix%20temp.webp?width=255&height=325&name=InnoApp%20What%20are%20my%20Quick%20Actions-brix%20temp.webp)

#### Record Vineyard Event

Jump directly into a menu of recordable events, then select your vineyard and block to record events out in the field.  See more information on Recording Vineyard Events [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vineyard-mobile-app?hsLang=en#actions).
