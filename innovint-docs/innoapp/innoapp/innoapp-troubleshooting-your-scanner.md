### Check your permissions

#### iPhone

1. Ensure your browser application permissions are updated.

- - You need to allow Safari (the recommended browser for installing InnoApp to your homescreen) access to your camera:
    - Open the Settings app.  Tap on Safari > Camera
    - Confirm that "Ask" or "Allow" is checked

#### Android

1. Ensure your browser application permissions are updated.

- You need to allow Google Chrome (the recommended browser for installing InnoApp to your homescreen) access to your camera:
  - Open Settings. Tap on Apps
    ![Troubleshooting the Scanner-android app](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-android%20app.webp?width=152&height=263&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-android%20app.webp)
  - Select Chrome > Permissions > Camera
    - choose "Allow" or "Allow only only while using the app":![Troubleshooting the Scanner-android allow camera](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-android%20allow%20camera.webp?width=226&height=255&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-android%20allow%20camera.webp)  or ![Troubleshooting the Scanner-cam permission](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-cam%20permission.webp?width=221&height=266&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-cam%20permission.webp)

2.  Check that your Camera settings allow QR code scanning:

- On Pixel phones - if the Pixel Camera app is unable to scan the QR code, it could be because the *Google Lens* *suggestions* feature is disabled. To enable it, launch Camera, tap on the settings icon in the top-left corner, and select More settings. Under General, toggle on the button for Google Lens suggestions.![Troubleshooting the Scanner-pixel camera](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-pixel%20camera.webp?width=592&height=399&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-pixel%20camera.webp)
- On Galaxy phones - check the Camera settings to ensure the camera is set to scan QR codes. To check, launch Camera, tap on the settings icon in the top-left corner, and ensure Scan QR codes is turned on.
  ![Troubleshooting the Scanner-galaxy cam icon](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-galaxy%20cam%20icon.webp?width=211&height=352&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-galaxy%20cam%20icon.webp)   ![Troubleshooting the Scanner-galaxy toggle scan](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-galaxy%20toggle%20scan.webp?width=189&height=353&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-galaxy%20toggle%20scan.webp)

3. Occasionally, users accidentally block the camera from the mobile.innovint.us when using the app.  If you are still getting a black screen, check Chrome's allowed website settings:

- Open Chrome, and at the upper righthand side, tap on the three dot menu
- Select Settings, then Site Settings (under the Advanced sub-header), then Camera
- Check that mobile.innovint.us is on the Allowed list:
  ![Troubleshooting the Scanner-chrome site settings](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-chrome%20site%20settings.webp?width=154&height=406&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-chrome%20site%20settings.webp)   ![Troubleshooting the Scanner-chrome cam](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-chrome%20cam.webp?width=194&height=394&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-chrome%20cam.webp)   ![Troubleshooting the Scanner-chrome perm](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-chrome%20perm.webp?width=248&height=397&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-chrome%20perm.webp)

### Check your operating system specifications

#### iPhone

Recommended specs:  for best performance we now recommend iOS17, with a minimum iOS16, working with the Safari browser.

This means that the last six years of Apple devices will run InnoApp without an issue (iPhone 8, iPad 5 and newer). You may still try to run InnoApp on older devices but we don't officially support them, or fix any issues specific to an older operating system.

#### Android

Recommended specs: operating system of Android 7.0 or higher with the latest version of Google Chrome (the recommended browser app to access InnoApp).

### Reset and refresh

If you are having trouble, it's always a good idea to try these steps on all devices and systems:

1. Log out and log back in using the **Log out** button at the bottom of the homescreen

![Troubleshooting the Scanner-logout](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-logout.webp?width=195&height=349&name=Troubleshooting%20the%20Scanner-logout.webp)

You cannot change or reset a forgotten password on InnoApp. Please use the desktop app to [reset your password](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/en-us/articles/212950563-how-do-i-change-or-reset-my-password-?hsLang=en) if required.

2. Uninstall and reinstall the app. Uninstall the app as you would any app - normally tapping and holding the app icon will bring up the uninstall option. Follow the steps [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/highlights-of-the-new-mobile-experience?hsLang=en#gettingstarted) to reinstall InnoApp to your homescreen.
