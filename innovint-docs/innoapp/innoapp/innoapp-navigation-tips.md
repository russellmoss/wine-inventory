#### **Offline Mode Indicator**

The little cloud in the top right corner will show you if you are online or offline. If the cloud is yellow, you aren't connected to the internet, but you can still view and record information. As you record offline data, we store it until you reconnect, and then it uploads automatically. You can see the number of events or actions stored until they are synched:

#### **Your account toggle and the Main Module Menu**

If you have access to multiple wineries, choose from the dropdown menu to navigate between them.

![InnoApp Navigation and Tips-toggle main menu](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-toggle%20main%20menu.webp?width=203&height=194&name=InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-toggle%20main%20menu.webp)

The main explorers available for InnoApp can be seen in the middle of your screen after logging in: Lots, Vessels, Vineyards, and Work Orders.

*Lots*: View all wine lots, fruit lots and case good lots (archived and unarchived). Use the search bar with any text string to quickly find a lot. See more info on the Lots capabilities [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/lots?hsLang=en).

*Vessels*: See a full list of your vessels (archived and unarchived). See more info on the Vessels capabilities [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vessel-explorer-page?hsLang=en).

*Vineyards*: Access your vineyard and block data, and record analysis and events. See more info on the Vineyard capabilities [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vineyard-mobile-app?hsLang=en).

*Work Orders:* See a list of open, started and submitted work orders; view, complete and submit tasks. See more on work order capability [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/beta?hsLang=en).

#### **Favorites and Recents**

- *What is a Favorite?*
  If you tap the star on a lot, vessel, or vineyard, it will stay pinned to your homescreen in the Favorites section. It will also allow for full data access to that item when you are offline.

![InnoApp Navigation and Tips-favs](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-favs.webp?width=303&height=107&name=InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-favs.webp)

These "favorited" items download and retain the full item history whenever the app is opened - and this data will remain fully accessible even when you are offline. What does this mean? If you are headed out to a group of vineyards with no cell access, you should favorite those vineyards before you go, and then you will be able to view the entire set of current vineyard data (blocks, events, analyses, etc) the entire time that you are offline. If an item is not favorited, you can still record actions offline, but you will not be able to view the entire dataset attached to that item.

If you are headed into an area of the cellar with no cell access, make sure to favorite your open work orders before you go!  If the work order hasn't been recently loaded or favorited, InnoVint won't be able to access the task details offline.

![InnoApp Navigation and Tips-fav list](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-fav%20list.webp?width=314&height=185&name=InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-fav%20list.webp)

- *What are Recents?*  The last 10 viewed items (Lots, Vessels, Vineyards or Work Orders). A recently accessed item is not necessarily a favorite. If an item is listed in your Recents, you will also have increased offline access to the Lot, Vessel, Vineyard or Work Order details.

#### **Quick Actions**

Quick Actions are the fastest way to enter data into InnoApp. Yes, you can access actions and analysis via [Lot](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/lots?hsLang=en#details), [Vessel](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vessel-explorer-page?hsLang=en#Details) and [Vineyard](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/vineyard-mobile-app?hsLang=en#details) or Block pages, but you can also jump straight into an action right on your main screen! Tag or update vessel locations, Record analysis on a Lot or Vineyard, or enter your Vineyard events right from these shortcuts. See a deeper dive into Quick Action descriptions [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/mobile-app-what-are-my-quick-actions?hsLang=en).

#### **Account Settings**

##### **On InnoApp**

**Log out or Change Language**

If you need to log out on a shared device, or want to check out the app in Spanish or Greek, use the options at the bottom of the home screen.

**![InnoApp Navigation and Tips-log out change lang](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-log%20out%20change%20lang.webp?width=210&height=87&name=InnoApp%20Navigation%20and%20Tips-log%20out%20change%20lang.webp)**

##### **On InnoVint desktop**

**Set Work Order requirements for vessel check-off**

- You can opt to require your team to check off vessels:

  - in order to start a work order task, or
  - in order to complete a task.
- You can also opt to require a specific method for vessel check-off:

  - allow the team to tap or scan vessels to check off
  - require the team to scan a vessel QR code to check off
- ![Settings_InnoApp Checkoff](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/Settings_InnoApp%20Checkoff.png?width=670&height=355&name=Settings_InnoApp%20Checkoff.png)
