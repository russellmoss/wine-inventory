# Navigating SUPPLY

The top and left-hand navigation bars are always available on the edges of  your screen when you are logged into the SUPPLY web platform. The top bar gives you quick access to switch between SUPPLY accounts, record an inventory action, or manage your profile.

Use the left-hand navigation bar to access the SKU Explorer or the TTB export.

### Top Navigation Bar

#### Organization Dropdown

If you have access to multiple organizations in SUPPLY, click on “Select organization”  to open the dropdown. Click on the organization name you want to view to switch accounts.

![SUPPLY_Top nav bar_organization dropdown](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/SUPPLY_Top%20nav%20bar_organization%20dropdown.webp?width=498&height=139&name=SUPPLY_Top%20nav%20bar_organization%20dropdown.webp)

#### Record inventory action

Click on the **Record inventory action** button to access the action list dropdown. Easily record inventory actions for multiple SKUs at once.

![SUPPLY_Top nav bar_Record inventory action dropdown](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/SUPPLY_Top%20nav%20bar_Record%20inventory%20action%20dropdown.webp?width=688&height=137&name=SUPPLY_Top%20nav%20bar_Record%20inventory%20action%20dropdown.webp)

#### User Profile

Click on the profile icon to view your login details, change your password, or log out of SUPPLY. Find out more about accessing your InnoVint account [here](https://support.innovint.us/hc/en-us/accessing-your-innovintaccount?hsLang=en).

![image-png-May-01-2025-10-46-47-4971-PM](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/image-png-May-01-2025-10-46-47-4971-PM.png?width=269&height=155&name=image-png-May-01-2025-10-46-47-4971-PM.png)

### Left-hand Navigation Bar

#### SKU Explorer

Click on the wine bottle icon in the left-hand navigation bar to access the SKU Explorer.

![SUPPLY_Side Nav Bar_Wine bottle](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/SUPPLY_Side%20Nav%20Bar_Wine%20bottle.webp?width=68&height=157&name=SUPPLY_Side%20Nav%20Bar_Wine%20bottle.webp)

#### TTB Export

Click on the report icon in the left-hand navigation bar to access the TTB export.

![SUPPLY_Side nav bar_Report icon](https://support.innovint.us/hs-fs/hubfs/SUPPLY_Side%20nav%20bar_Report%20icon.webp?width=68&height=157&name=SUPPLY_Side%20nav%20bar_Report%20icon.webp)

#### Using SUPPLY on mobile devices

SUPPLY works on mobile devices too!  All screens in SUPPLY will adapt to the screen size of your device.

You can access SUPPLY from your phone or tablet by entering [supply.innovint.us](http://supply.innovint.us) into your browser just like you would on desktop. All SUPPLY screens and functionality are retained in the mobile and tablet views - including actions and reports!
