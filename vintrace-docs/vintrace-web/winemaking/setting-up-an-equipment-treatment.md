# Setting Up an Equipment Treatment

Equipment treatments are actions that you perform on your equipment that you want to track. Examples include:

- Checking tanks
- Cleaning tanks
- Adding or removing staves from tanks
- [Sanitizing tanks](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301335169428)

You can add an equipment treatment from the Winery Setup window (Setup Options > Treatments > Treatment (Equipment)).

To add an equipment treatment:

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329206710164) Set Up in the sidebar.
2. Click Treatments, or search for *treatment*.
3. From the Equipment Treatments tile, click Configure.
4. Click New Equipment Treatments. The Equipment Treatment Definition window displays.

![Equipment_Treatment_Definition_Create_20221108.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329239560340)

5. Specify the details for the equipment treatment.
6. Click Save.

Users with a multi-winery license can specify which product treatments are available at each winery. Refer to our [Configuration for Multi-Winery Support article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301304791316) for details.
