# Scanning Vessels

You can [print QR codes on labels](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303321067412) and attach them to your vessels.

For information on recommended printers and labels, refer to our [Printing Recommendations for QR Codes article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303299842708).

Once the QR codes are attached to your vessels, you can scan them to view the details of the wine in the vessel. You can then [add tags](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301386340244), [view and record labs](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301385573012), and [add notes](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301354570132) to the wine.

To scan a vessel from the mobile app:

1. Tap the barcode icon from the main screen.

![Barcode_Scanner_20201005.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329130884116)

2. Scan the code attached to your vessel.
