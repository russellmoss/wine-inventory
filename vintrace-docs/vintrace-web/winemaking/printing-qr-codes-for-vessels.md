# Printing QR Codes for Vessels

You can print QR codes that you can stick to your vessels. A sample QR code is shown below.

![Print_QR_codes_2.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328960199188)

You can [scan the QR code from the vintrace app](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303349951252) to view the vessel’s current contents. You can also [tag the wine](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301386340244), [view and record lab results](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301385573012), and [add notes and attachments](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301354570132).

For information on recommended printers and labels, refer to our [Printing Recommendations for QR Codes article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303299842708).

To print QR codes for vessels:

1. Click ![Vessels_Menu_Option_20200402.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328935793556) Vessels in the sidebar. The [Vessels page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301344827924) displays.
2. [Search](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301344204308) for and select the vessels that you want to generate a QR code for.
3. From the Actions menu, select Print QR Codes, then select the size.

![Actions_-_Print_QR_Codes_20200403.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328960271252)
