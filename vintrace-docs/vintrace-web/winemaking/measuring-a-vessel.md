# Measuring a Vessel

In addition to recording a vessel's volume, you can specify the loss or gain reason.

![Measurement_-_Loss_and_Loss_Reason_20221031.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328605440916)

From the [product page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303310460948), you can click the ![Measurement_Icon_20221031.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328577435412) measurement icon.

![Product_-_Measurement_Icon_20221031.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328577480724)
You can also click the ![Operations_Icon_20200407.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328613571092) operations icon from the [Vessels page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301344827924), then select Measurement.

![Record_an_Operation_-_Measurement_20221031.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328613543316)
