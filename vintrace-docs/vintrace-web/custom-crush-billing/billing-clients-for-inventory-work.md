# Billing Clients for Inventory Work

Billing for inventory work will be charged to the stock item’s service order. Be sure that you’ve specified a service order by setting the stock item’s Order setting.

![Update_17vintraceCS_with_Order_20200719.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328989671572)

You’ll also want to ensure that you’ve selected one or more stock actions for the billing item.

![Create_Item_-_Stock_Links_-_Stock_Actions_20200719.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328989691924)

When you complete one of the selected stock actions on the stock item, the Service Charges window displays.

For example, a stock dispatch was performed on multiple stock items that are linked to a service order and billing item. The Service Charges window displays each batch’s billing by service order. After you’ve generated your charges, you can create invoices for them.

[![](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2018/12/Inventory-7.jpg)](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2018/12/Inventory-7.jpg)

After you have generated your charges, you can create invoices for them.
