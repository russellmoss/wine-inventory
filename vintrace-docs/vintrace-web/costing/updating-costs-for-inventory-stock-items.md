# Updating Costs for Inventory Stock Items

If you discover a data entry error for cost or quantity, or receive a revision such as a credit memo, you can easily update the costs for additives, dry goods, or packaged goods.

If you’ve created a purchase order, especially for dry goods, you can update or change costs on received items by editing the purchase order.

## Correcting Stock Receival from Purchase Order

Locate and open the purchase order by doing the following:

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328907115412) More Options from the sidebar.
2. From the Purchase tile, click Manage Purchase Orders.
3. Search for the purchase order by adjusting the date range and selecting the Fulfilled checkbox.

![Purchase_Orders_-_Fulfilled_20200625.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328898947732)

4. Click Search.
5. Click the purchase order.

Correct the purchase order by doing the following:

1. On the Details tab, edit the details as needed.
2. Select the Stock Receivals tab.

![Update_Stock_Purchase_Order_-_Stock_Receivals_Tab_20200625.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328877081620)

3. Click the relevant receival.
4. From the Action Receive window, click Correct.

![Action_Receive_-_Purchase_-Correct_Button_VPO54_20200625.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328877104020)

5. Correct the Received amount, and/or Price as needed.
6. In the Reason for Correction field, enter an explanation for the correction.

![Action_Receive_-_Correction_-_Purchase_VPO54_20200625.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328887732372)

7. Click Save.

## Correcting Stock Receival from Stock Item History

If you didn't use a purchase order, you can correct the unit costs and/or quantity by correcting the stock receival.

1. Use vintrace’s Quick Search to find the item.
2. From the Stock Item Overview window, select the History tab.

![Stock_Item_Overview_-_History_CRK-Porto_20200625.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328916179092)

3. Adjust the date range to find the receival.
4. Click View.
5. From the Action Receive window, click Correct.

![Action_Receive_-_Purchase_-Correct_Button_VPO54_20200625.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328877104020)

6. Correct the Received amount, and/or Price as needed.
7. In the Reason for Correction field, enter an explanation for the correction.
8. Click Save.
