# Printing QR Codes for Storage Areas and Bays

You can print QR codes that you can use to label storage areas and bays in your winery. A sample QR code is shown below.

![QR_Code_Storage_Area_Bay.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329213775764)

You can [scan the QR code from the vintrace app](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301320474388-Scanning-Barcodes-and-QR-Codes-on-the-Mobile-App) to select the storage area or bay on the Move and Adjustment jobs.

For information on recommended printers and labels, refer to our [Printing Recommendations for QR Codes article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303299842708-Printing-Recommendations-for-QR-Codes).

## Print QR codes for storage areas

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329251164692) Set up in the sidebar.
2. Click Locations.
3. From the Storage areas tile, click Configure.
4. Select the storage areas that you want to generate a QR code for.

![Print_Storage_Area_QR_Codes_1_.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329230137748)

5. Click Print QR Codes. The Print QR Codes window displays.

![Print_Storage_Area_QR_Codes_2.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329251232532)

6. From the Print Preset list, select *Areas only*.
7. Enter a number in the Copies field.
8. From the Label Type list, select the label size.
9. Click Download.

## Print QR codes for storage areas and bays

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329251164692) Set up in the sidebar.
2. Click Locations.
3. From the Storage areas tile, click Configure.
4. Select the storage areas that you want to generate a QR code for.
5. Click Print QR Codes. The Print QR Codes window displays.

![Print_Storage_Area_Bay_QR_Codes_2.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329242955412)

6. From the Print Preset list, select *Areas & Bays*.
7. Enter a number in the Copies field.
8. From the Label Type list, select the label size.
9. Next enter Bays as a comma separated list and press Enter.
10. Click Download.
