# Print Barcodes for Stock Items

You can print stock item labels that you can use to stick on the pallet for the stock items. A sample label is shown below.

![Stock_Item_Label_Sample.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328901237268)

You can [scan the barcode from the vintrace app](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301320474388-Scanning-Barcodes-and-QR-Codes-on-the-Mobile-App) to view details including the location, history, wine details and item components and complete Move and Adjustment jobs.

For information on recommended printers and labels, refer to our [Printers and Labels article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303330395284-Recommended-Printers-and-Labels).

To print barcodes for stock items:

1. Search for and open the stock item from the [Quick Search](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301339319700-Navigating-vintrace).
2. From the Reports menu, select Stock Item Label.

![Generate_Stock_Item_Label.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328878899988)

The stock item labels can also be generated from the Stock Search window:

1. Click ![](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/49846035122196) Inventory in the sidebar.
2. Search for the inventory items.

![Generate_Multiple_Stock_Item_Labels.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328896187412)

3. Click on Item Labels.
4. Open the stock item and select Stock Item Label from the Reports menu.
