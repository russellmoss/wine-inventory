# Entering a Physical Inventory/Stock Take for Inventory Stock Items

Be sure that all work orders have been completed prior to entering stock intake into vintrace so that your counts are accurate.

## Printing a Stock Count Sheet

You can print a hardcopy of the Stock Count Sheet for the items that you're taking a physical inventory.

To print the Stock Count Sheet:

1. Click ![Inventory_Menu_Option_20200406.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328922747540) Inventory from the sidebar.
2. From the Stock Search window, search for the item.
3. From the Reports menu located at the bottom of the window, select Physical Inventory (Enhanced).

![Stock_Search_-_Reports_Menu_-_Physical_Inventory_Enhanced_20200518.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328922834836)

The Stock Count Sheet displays. You can print the Stock Count Sheet to record your inventory.

![Stock_Count_Sheet_20200518.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328922890900)

## Entering Stock Count Adjustments

After you’ve taken inventory, you can adjust your stock counts by doing the following:

1. Click ![Inventory_Menu_Option_20200406.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328922747540) Inventory from the sidebar.
2. From the Stock Search window, search for the item.
3. Click Enter Physical Inventory.

![Stock_Search_-_Enter_Physical_Inventory_Button_20200518.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328922858644)

4. Enter the physical counts in the Actual field of the Action Adjustment window. If you don’t want to adjust the count for an item, click the ![X_in_Gray_Circle_20200330.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328913965076) beside it. The default Reason for each item will be set to Stocktake, but you can change this if you’d like.

![Action_Adjustment_-_Physical_Inventory_20200518.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328905664148)

5. Click Save. The stock levels will be adjusted using the date in the Action Adjustment window.
