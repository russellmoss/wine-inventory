# Moving Stock

The Move operation in vintrace allows you to transfer inventory items from one physical location to another. US customers can use this operation to move bonded items to tax-paid storage areas to record on their TTB report.

1. Click ![Inventory_Menu_Option_20200406.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329177463700) Inventory in the sidebar.
2. From the Operations menu, select Move.

![Stock_Search_-_Operations_-_Move_20200512.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329168284436)

3. Enter the following details in the General tab of the Action Move window:

![Action_Move_Example_20200512.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329163296916)

- Stock Items — Search for the stock items to move. Click Add Line to add additional items.
- Quantity — The amount of the item to move.
- Route From — Click the ![Forklift_20200511.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329131811988) to select the area where stock is being moved from. If the stock is in a single area, that area will be automatically selected.
- Route To — Click the ![Forklift_20200511.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329131811988)to select the area where stock is being to. To move the stock to multiple locations, click Add Line to select another location.

![Route_Stock_to_Storage_Area_-_Multiple_Locations_20200512.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329177434004)

4. Click OK.
