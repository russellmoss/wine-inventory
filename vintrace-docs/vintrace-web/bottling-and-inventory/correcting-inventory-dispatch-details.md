# Correcting Inventory Dispatch Details

To correct the details of an inventory dispatch:

1. [Search for the dispatch](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301313789460).
2. Click the dispatch.
3. Click View/Edit Stock.
4. From the Stock Item Overview window, select the History tab.
5. Click View beside the stock item.
6. Click Correct.

![Action_Dispatch_-_Correct_Button_20200511.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329051181716)

7. Correct the dispatch details. If you’re correcting the Dispatch To details, click Reset; this allows you to search for a party in your address book, or add a new party.
8. Enter a reason for the correction.
9. Click Save. Don’t click Now + Save as this may update the dispatch date.

The Bill of Lading Declaration window displays with the corrected details.
