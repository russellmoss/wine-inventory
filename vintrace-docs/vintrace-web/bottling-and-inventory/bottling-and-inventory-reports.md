# Bottling and Inventory Reports

The following bottling and inventory reports are available in vintrace:

- [Bulk Stock Receipt Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301367238036) — The Bulk Stock Receipt Report shows all [bulk intakes](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303303281428) for the specified date range.
- [Inventory Stock Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303326373396) — The Inventory Stock Report details your [stock](https://support.winery-software.com/hc/en-us/sections/360000176075-Bottling-and-Inventory) as of the specified date.
- [Inventory Summary Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301306729236) — The Inventory Summary Report shows a summary of the selected stock category during the specified date range.
- [Packaged/Manufactured Stock Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301367143444) — The Packaged/Manufactured Stock Report shows all [packaging, bottling](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303327186836), and [manufacturing](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303341990548) operations during the specified date range.
- [Stock Dispatch Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301330369684) — The Stock Dispatch Report shows the [dispatch](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303319075988) history for your stock during the specified date range.
- [Stock Movement Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301321425044) — The Stock Movement Report shows all stock that [moved between locations](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303355248916) during the specified date range.
- [Stock Receipt Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301367256724) — The Stock Receipt report shows all [inventory that you’ve received](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303350382356) during the specified date range.

## Accessing the Reports

To access these reports:

1. Click ![Reports_Menu_Option_20200406.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329244849044) Reports in the sidebar.
2. Click Inventory.

![Winery_Reports_-_Inventory_20201217.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329253248660)

The reports are displayed in the following order:

- [Inventory Stock Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303326373396)
- [Stock Receipt Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301367256724)
- [Bulk Stock Receipt Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301367238036)
- [Stock Dispatch Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301330369684)
- [Packaged/Manufactured Stock Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301367143444)
- [Stock Movement Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301321425044)
- [Inventory Summary Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301306729236)

Refer to each report’s article for details on its available filters.
