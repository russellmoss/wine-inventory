# Showing Dates for a Wine's Lab Analysis

There are two options for how a wine’s latest labs are displayed on the General and Lab tabs of the Product page.

The *How Recent the Result was Recorded* option displays the amount of time since the wine’s latest lab results.

![Product_-_Latest_Labs_-_How_Recent_20200618.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329148623252)

The *Date and Time* option displays the dates and times of the latest lab results.

![Product_-_Latest_Labs_-_Date_Time_20200618.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329181652500)

## Changing How Lab Result Dates are Displayed

To change how the lab result dates are displayed on the General and Lab tabs of the Product page:

1. View the wine’s Product page. Refer to our [Vessels Page article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301344827924) to learn more about accessing your wines.
2. Click the ![Gear_Green_Icon_20200417.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329200394772).

![Gear_Icon_Location_20200618.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329181517844)

3. At the bottom of the Customise Product Metrics window, select the option you’d like to use for displaying lab results.

![Date_and_Time_20200618.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329158177684)
