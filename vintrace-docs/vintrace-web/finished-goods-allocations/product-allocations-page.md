# Product Allocations Page

The Product Allocations page lets you manage the supply and demand process. To access the Product Allocations page, click ![Product_Allocation_Icon_20220111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328664301460) Products in the sidebar.

At the top of the Product Allocations page are filters that let you refine what’s displayed on the page. The  ![Manage_Allocations_Icon_20220111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328648036372) manage allocations icon displays the [allocations table](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301328118932) so that you can [allocate your bulk wine to products](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303293781780). The ![Plus_in_Green_Circle_20200319.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328638427668) plus icon lets you [add new products](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301350767380).

![Product_Page_-_Filters_and_Icons_20220112.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328629482772)

The Name column displays the vintage and product name. You can click the name to view and edit the [product allocation’s details](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301350652948).

![Product_Page_-_Name_20220111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328664367892)

## Filtering the Product Allocations Page

The filters at the top of the Product Allocations page let you refine what’s displayed.

You can search for a product’s vintage and name, description, or item code by entering the value in the Quick Search field.

![Product_Page_-_Quick_Search_20220111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328636134036)
If you specify more than one filter, only products that meet all the filters will display.
