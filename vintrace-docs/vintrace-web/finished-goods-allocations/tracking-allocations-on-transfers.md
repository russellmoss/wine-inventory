# Tracking Allocations on Transfers

As wine is produced, moved, blended, and losses made, you can track allocated volumes and avoid shortfalls for products.

By default, the Proportional Allocation option is selected. This option auto calculates the fulfilled amount of allocated and unallocated volume according to their ration.

![Racking_-_Proportional_Allocation_20201014.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328638521236)

You can change the allocation option by clicking Proportional Allocation, then selecting the option you prefer.

![Manage_Allocation_-_List_Options_20201014.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328629529108)
