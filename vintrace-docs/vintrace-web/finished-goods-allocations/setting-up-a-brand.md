# Setting Up a Brand

This functionality will be available to all accounts starting with [vintrace 9.6.1b0](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303261607828)1.

A brand provides a way to organize your products. Each brand can have multiple products, but each product can only be associated with one brand. However, a wine can be allocated to multiple products. This means that a wine can be associated with multiple brands.

To set up a brand:

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328593611412) Set Up in the sidebar.
2. Click Classification, or search for *brand*.

![Search_for_Brand_20221109.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328617058580)

3. From the Brand tile, click Configure. The Brand window displays.

![Brand_Create_20221109.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328625041044)

4. Enter a name for the brand. You may also enter an optional description.
5. Click Save.

After you've added brands, you can do the following.

On the Vessels page, you can:

- [Customize the Vessels page to include the Brand and Desc/Brand (i.e., brand description) columns](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001505616#ChangingtheColumnsDisplayed).
- [Filter the Vessels page by the brand and brand description](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001550655#FilteringtheVesselsPage).

![Vessels_Page_-_Brand_Column_20231203.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328599824276)

On the Product Allocations page, you can:

- Specify the brand when you [add a new product.](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/4413543988239-Adding-a-Product-and-Its-Demand)
- [Filter the Product Allocations page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/4413602205327-Product-Allocations-Page#FilteringtheProductAllocationsPage) by brand.
- Display the brand on the [Product Allocation Details page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/4413615829135).

In the [Bulk Wine Search,](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000822676)you can filter and display the brand and brand description.

On the [Tank Map](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301346088340), you can view the brand.
