# Archiving a Product

This functionality is available starting with [vintrace 9.20.1.](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/10614156453391-Version-9-20-1)

The ability to archive products is only available to users with the [Can Adjust Allocation Product Status permission](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000813755-Roles-and-Permissions#Permissions).

You can [archive a product from the list on the Product Allocations page](#archiving_product_page), or while [viewing a specific product](#archiving_product_details).

## Archiving a Product While Viewing a Product

1. Click ![Products Icon 20200715.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328756486036) products in the sidebar.
2. Click the product name.

   ![](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328783324052)
   *Please note that a product can only be archived when the Demand, In production and Produced fields have no value.*
3. From the Status tile, click the ![Pencil Gray Icon 20200414.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328782573204) pencil icon.

The Edit Product Status window displays.

4. From the Status list, select *Archived*.

![Edit Product Status - Archived 20240821.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328741576724)

5. Click Save. Product has now been archived.

## Archiving a Product from the Product Allocations Page

To archive a product from the list on the [Product Allocations page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301319185940):

1. Click ![Products Icon 20200715.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328756486036) products in the sidebar.
2. Click the ![Three Vertical Dots 20200623.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328765427220)to the right of the product.

![Product Page - Options 20240821.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328783181844)

3. Select Change Status.

![Product Page - Options - Change Status 20240821.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328741390228)

The Edit Product Status window displays.

4. From the Status list, select *Archived*.

![Edit Product Status - Archived 20240821.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328741576724)

5. Click Save.
