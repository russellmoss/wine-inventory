# Adding a Product and Its Demand

The ability to add products is only available to users with the [Can add / edit allocation products permission](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303349421588#Permissions).

To start tracking allocations, you’ll need to [create a new product](#h_01GGDHV5ZAP6HDJR1JXFBHA9DN). Next, you'll need to [specify the product's demand per vintage and supply](#h_01GGDHRPEH9DZ718T65E2DB0Q8).

## Adding a Product

To create a product:

1. Select ![Product_Allocation_Icon_20220111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329240352020) Products from the sidebar.

You can also create a product by selecting More Options from the sidebar, then clicking Manage Products from the Products tile.

2. From the Products page, click the ![Plus_in_Green_Circle_20200319.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329207497876) add icon. The New Product window displays.

![New_Product_20220111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329199044628)

3. Enter the following details for the product:

- Product name
- Vintage
- Owner
- Winery (if relevant)
- Brand (if enabled)

4. Click Save. The product you created displays in the Products page. You can now [specify the product's demand](#h_01GGDHRPEH9DZ718T65E2DB0Q8).

## Specifying a Product's Demand

1. From the Products page, click the product.
2. Select the Stock Allocation tab.
3. Click the ![Plus_in_White_Circle_20200715.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329228050836) add icon to add the demand for the product. A new line displays in the Stock Allocations table.
4. Specify the customer for the product.
5. Enter the demand in the Allocation Gal column.

![Entering_Demand_20201014.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329212212756)

The Summary adjusts to reflect the demand.

![Updated_Demand_20201014.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329222488340)
