# Viewing the Water Addition Percentage of the Total Resulting Volume in Vessel for a Wine

The % of Total column on the Adds tab of the [details page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303310460948) of wine or juice enables you to determine if more than 1% of water has been added to a vessel. The percentage is calculated based on the volume of water addition added divided by the total resulting volume in the vessel.

![Percentage_of_Total_Calculation_20220720.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328627405972)

To view the % of Total:

1. View a wine/juice by using the [Quick Search](https://support.winery-software.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001922876#QuickSearch), or by selecting a vessel from the [Vessels page](https://support.winery-software.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001550655-The-Vessels-Page).
2. Click on the Adds tab to view the Additive summary and the % of Total.

![Water_Additive_Percentage_12-02-22_1.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328627375508)

By default the percentage will be shown to 4 decimal places. This can be configured per [additive](#configure_decimal_places). This percentage will only be displayed for [additives](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000812775-Setting-Up-an-Additive) with a volumetric unit of addition.

## Configuring the Decimal Places for the % of Total Column

The % of Total column can be configured per additive to display the required number of decimal places for the percentage.

To configure the number of decimal places:

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328619441044) Set Up in the sidebar.
2. Click Winemaking.
3. From the Additives tile, click Configure.
4. Open the required additive. The Additive window displays.
5. In the % Precision field, specify the % precision for the additive.

![Water_Additive_Percentage_12-02-22_2.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328596000148)

6. Click Save.

If the % precision is not specified for the additive, the percentage will be shown to 4 decimal places on the Adds tab by default. When creating new [additives](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000812775-Setting-Up-an-Additive), the % precision will be defaulted to 4.
