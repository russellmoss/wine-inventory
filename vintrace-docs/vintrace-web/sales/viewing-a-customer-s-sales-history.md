# Viewing a Customer's Sales History

There are several ways to view a customer’s sales history in vintrace:

- [From the address book](#From_Address_Book)
- [From a sales order](#From_Sales_Order)
- [From the Sales Report](#From_Sales_Report)

## From the Address Book

To view a customer’s sales history from the address book:

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160675348) More Options in the sidebar.
2. From the Address Book tile, click Open Address Book.
3. Click Advanced beside the customer.
4. Click Account Summary.

![Organization_Update_-_Account_Summary_Link_20200818.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329115395092)

The Accounts window displays the customer’s sales history.

![Accounts_-_Sales_Orders_-_Stone_Church_Vintners_20200818.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160662548)

## From a Sales Order

To view a customer’s sales history from a sales order:

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160675348) More Options in the sidebar.
2. From the Sales tile, click Manage Purchase Orders.
3. Search for the sales order.
4. Select the sales order.
5. Click Account Summary.

![Update_Sales_Order_-_Account_Summary_Link_20200818.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329140579476)

The Accounts window displays the customer’s sales history.

![Accounts_-_Sales_Orders_-_Stone_Church_Vintners_20200818.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160662548)

## Using the Sales Report

To view a customer’s sales history from the Sales Report:

1. Click ![Reports_Menu_Option_20200406.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329128559380) Reports in the sidebar.
2. Select Sales. The Sales Report displays.

![Winery_Reports_-_Sales_-_Sales_Report_20200818.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329152080404)

3. From the Customer field, search for and select the customer.

![Sales_Report_-_Customer_20200818.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329128627476)

4. Specify any other filters you’d like to use for the report.
5. Click Generate. A PDF of the customer’s sales history displays.

![Sales_Report_PDF_20200818.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329140639892)
