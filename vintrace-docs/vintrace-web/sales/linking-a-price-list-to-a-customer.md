# Linking a Price List to a Customer

When a price list is linked to a customer, the price list is automatically pre-selected when you [create a sales order](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303318150164) for the customer.

![Price_List_Linked_to_Customer_20200810.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329028554388)

To link a price list to a customer:

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329069492116) More Options in the sidebar.
2. From the Address Book tile, click Open Address Book.
3. Click Advanced beside the customer.

![Address_Book_-_Advanced_Button_20200810.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329051525780)

4. From the Primary Contact tab, in the Sales Price List field, select the price list that you want to link to the customer.

![Organization_Update_-_Sales_Price_List_20200810.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329046326036)

5. Click Save.
