# Multi-Currency Support for Costing, Sales, and Purchases

vintrace displays the local currency symbol based on the country that’s selected during your initial database setup.

![Product_-_Costs_in_US_Dollars_20200814.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329118898452)

However, vintrace supports currency symbols such as £, €, and ¥.

![Sales_Orders_-_Multi_Currency_Support_20200814.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329104114708)

## Using Different Currencies for Sales Orders

To use different currencies for sales orders, you’ll need to [set up a price list](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303325767316) that’s specific to the currency.

For example, if the Australian dollar is your local currency, but you want to enter a sales order in Euros, you’d need to set up a price list in Euros.

![Create_Sales_Price_List_-_Currency_for_Sales_Orders_20200813.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329082509716)

The invoice for sales orders will also be in the currency that’s used by the price list.

![Sales_Invoice_in_Euros_20200814.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329082547092)
