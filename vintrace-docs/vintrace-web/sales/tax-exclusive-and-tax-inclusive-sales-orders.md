# Tax Exclusive and Tax Inclusive Sales Orders

Whether a [sales order’s](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303318150164) items are tax exclusive or tax inclusive is determined by its [sales price list](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303325767316). Specifically, it’s specified in the price list’s Tax Policy setting.

![Create_Sales_Price_List_-_Tax_Policy_20200814.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328943165844)

When you add items to a sales order, you can hover over the Tax Amt to view the amount of tax that’s included in the sales price. The amount of tax is also displayed in the sales order’s sub-total.

![Create_Sales_Order_-_Tax_Amount_20200814.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328935275540)
