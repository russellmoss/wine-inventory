# Combining Barrels or Barrel Groups

The Multi Transfer (Many-to-One) operation lets you combine multiple barrels, or combine barrel groups into a single barrel group.

1. Click the ![Operations_Icon_20200407.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329037670804)Operations icon.
2. From the Transfers section, click Multi Transfer (Many-to-One).

![Record_an_Operation_-_Multi_Transfer_Many_to_One_20200430.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328998472340)

3. In the Source Vessels section of the Multi-Transfer (Many-to-One) window, select all of the barrels. To add additional vessels, click Add Line. If you've selected a barrel group, you can [use specific vessels from the group](#h_fc21ae7f-f24c-452d-9b77-ef1ae29c6159) by clicking the ![Select_Barrels_Icon_20200430.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329058488980) icon that's beside the vessel.

![General_-_Many_to_One_-_Source_20200430.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329037762324)

4. In the Destination Vessels section of the Multi-Transfer (Many-to-One) window, click ![Plus_in_Green_Circle_20200319.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329066591764) beside the Vessel field to [create a new barrel group](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301371378452).
5. After you’ve set up your barrel group, enter the transfer volume.
6. Click Save.

## Selecting Specific Barrels from a Barrel Group

If you’ve selected a barrel group, but only want to use some of the vessels from the group, do the following from Barrel Group List window:

1. Select the barrels.
2. Set the Fill to *Empty* on the barrels. Be sure to confirm that the Volume matches your expectations.

![Many_to_One_-_Barrel_Group_List_20200430.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329066562964)

3. Click OK.
