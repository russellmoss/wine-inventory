# Exporting Barrel Details

You can export a CSV of your barrel details from [the Vessels page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301344827924).

To export barrel details:

1. [Customize the Vessels page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301323976084) to include the barrel details that you want to export.
2. Click the ![Download_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329107184404) download icon located in the lower right of the Vessels page.
3. Click Barrel Details.

![Exporting_Barrel_Details_20200504.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329135895444)

4. Select the export option:

- All — exports the details for all of your barrels
- This Page — exports the details for the barrels displayed on the page that you’re currently viewing.
