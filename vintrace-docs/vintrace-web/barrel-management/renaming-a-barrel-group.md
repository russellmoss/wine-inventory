# Renaming a Barrel Group

You can view your barrel groups from the Winery Setup window (Setup Options > Vessels > Barrel Group). The list displays the barrel group, its capacity, number of barrels and location.

![Barrel_Groups_20200428.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328935240340)

To rename a barrel group:

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328935227796) Set Up in the sidebar.
2. Click Vessels.
3. From the Barrel Group tile, click Configure.
4. Click the barrel group that you want to rename. Be sure to select the barrel group since there may be multiple barrel groups with a similar name.
5. In the Barrel Group Code field, enter the barrel group’s name.

![Barrel_Group_Update_-_Name_20200428.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328935216916)

6. Click Save.
