# Customizing the Vessels Page

You can customize [the Vessels page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301344827924) by [changing the columns that are displayed](#h_01EH7T6AD8X1A8PHW1YKRXGV5Y), and their [display order](#h_01EH7T6M0MX0YWAX5GFEHPGTDH).

## Changing the Columns Displayed

To change the details displayed on the Vessels page:

1. Click the ![Gear_Green_Icon_20200417.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328613934484) Configure icon that's located to the right of the column headers.
2. Select the details you want displayed on the Vessels page.
3. Click X to close the Select Columns window.

![Changing_Columns_Displayed_20200504.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328621917460)

## Changing the Display Order of Columns

To change the order of the columns displayed on the Vessels page, click the column header and drag it to its new position.

![Changing_Column_Order_20200902.gif](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328605802644)
