# Redistillation

Redistillation produces more spirits from spirits.

![Diagram - Redistillation 20231031.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328755589524)

To redistill spirits, record a one-to-many transfer. When recording the transfer, the source vessel will be spirits in an *Under 190,* or *190 and Over* tax class that’s in the Production account.

The destination vessels for the transfer will be in the Storage - Domestic account. The spirits in the destination vessels will have a resulting tax class of either *Under 190*, or *190 and Over*.
