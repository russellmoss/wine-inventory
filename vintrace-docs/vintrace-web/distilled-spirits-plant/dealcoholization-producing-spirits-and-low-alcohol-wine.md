# Dealcoholization: Producing Spirits and Low-Alcohol Wine

This functionality is available starting with [vintrace 9.4.3](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303276816020), but not enabled by default. If you would like to use this functionality, please contact our support team.

Be sure to complete the [configuration steps for a Distilled Spirits Plant (DSP)](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301312232852) before starting this workflow.

![Diagram - Dealc Producing Spirits and Low-Alc Wine 20230904.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328779225620)

After the aromas have been removed from the wine and you’re ready to produce the low-alcohol wine and spirits, record a one-to-many transfer.

When recording the transfer:

- Select a source vessel in the Production account for the Wine (*Column l*) tax class that has had the aromas removed.
- Be sure to select empty destination vessel(s) in the storage - domestic account.
- At least one of the destination vessels will be for the spirits in the *Column i or j* tax class.
- The other destination vessel(s) will be for the low-alcohol wine in the Wine (*Column l*) tax class.
- Be sure to record the alcohol percentage for the low-alcohol wine.

Both the spirits and low-alcohol wine produced will be moved to the storage - domestic account. The DSP tax events will be included in the [TTB Monthly Report of Production Operations](https://www.ttb.gov/images/pdfs/forms/f511040.pdf) and the [Monthly Report of Storage Operations](https://www.ttb.gov/images/pdfs/forms/f511011.pdf).
