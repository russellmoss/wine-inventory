# Xero Integration

By connecting vintrace to your Xero account, you can push Sales, Purchases, Refunds and Client Billing invoices out to Xero for invoicing or payment.

IMPORTANT: This connection is a one way connection - vintrace can push data to Xero but not the other way around.

IMPORTANT: No cost information (COGS) is pushed to Xero, only the end price of the good or service being sold/purchased/refunded.

To integrate vintrace with Xero, you'll need to have someone from your winery with access to Xero to log into vintrace to complete the following steps.

1. Click ![vin_setup_icon.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/48158158575892) Set Up in the sidebar.
2. Click Accounts.
3. From the Accounts Integration tile, click Configure.

![account_integration_configure.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/48158161911700)

4. From the Xero tile, click Connect.

![xero_account_configure.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/48158161911828)

5. Enter your Xero email address and password.
6. Click Log In.
   ![Xero_3.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329218388372)
7. If the Xero organisation selected is the correct one for your winery, click Allow Access.
   ![Xero_4.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329189262484)

When vintrace is successfully connected to Xero, the Xero tile in vintrace displays Connected as: with the Xero organisation name below.

![xero_demo_company.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/48158161912084)

You can access the following options for Xero by clicking the ![vin_xero_setup.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/48158161912212) in the Xero tile.

- Decimal precision settings (recommended to set to Two Decimal Precision)
- Link client billing items to account
- Open accounts setup
- Test connectivity

![xero_settings_options.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/48158161912468)
