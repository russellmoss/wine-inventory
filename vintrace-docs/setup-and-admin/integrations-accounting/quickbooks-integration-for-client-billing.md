# QuickBooks Integration for Client Billing

To link QuickBooks to vintrace:

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329127885716) Set Up in the sidebar.
2. Click Accounts.
3. From the Accounts Integration tile, click Configure.
4. From the QuickBooks tile, click Connect.

![QB_1a_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329127906964)

You’ll be redirected to the QuickBooks login page.

5. Enter your QuickBooks login information, then click Sign In.

![QB_2_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329139965076)

6. Click Connect.

![QB_3_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160118036)

Once you’re successfully connected to QuickBooks, the Link Items to Account form displays. This form lets you link a revenue account to QuickBooks; you can then link billing items to the revenue account.

7. From the Account field, select the vintrace account that you want to link to QuickBooks.

![QB_5_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329114950292)

8. Click the QuickBooks icon beside the field to display a list of all your accounts.

![QB_5a_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160158356)

9. Select the checkboxes for billing items that you want to link to QuickBooks.

![QB_7.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329151346836)

10. Click Link to Account.

![QB_8.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160002452)

If you have multiple revenue accounts and items to link to QuickBooks, you can select Link Items to Account from vintrace. ![QB_9_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329160179988)

For information on linking vintrace and QuickBooks tax rates, refer to our [Linking QuickBooks Tax Rates article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303341350420). For details on syncing QuickBooks invoices, billing items, and customers, refer to our [Syncing Invoices and Billing Items to QuickBooks article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303355479188).
