# Linking QuickBooks Tax Rates

If you’ve [linked vintrace to QuickBooks](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303321099924) and your billing items are taxable, you’ll need to link the tax rate to QuickBooks.

To link your tax rates in vintrace to the tax rate in QuickBooks, you’ll need to [set the tax rate’s Linked Tax Rate ID](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303335629332) to the tax rate you have set up in QuickBooks.

![Linked_Tax_Rate_ID_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329139126036)

When you [set up a sales price list](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303325767316), be sure that its Default Tax Rate is set to the tax rate that’s linked to QuickBooks. [Sales orders](https://support.winery-software.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000823336-Managing-Sales-Orders) using that price list will be linked to the appropriate tax rate in QuickBooks.

![Tax_rate_1.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329127017108)

The diagram below illustrates the relationship between sales orders, price lists, and tax rates in vintrace with the tax rate in QuickBooks.

![Linking_Tax_Rates_to_QuickBooks_20201125.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329150606612)

For details on syncing QuickBooks invoices, billing items, and customers, refer to our [Syncing Invoices and Billing Items to QuickBooks](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303355479188) article.
