# Syncing Purchase Orders with Xero

If you’ve [integrated vintrace with Xero](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303310784660), you can sync purchase orders from vintrace to Xero.

Be sure that you've also [set up the asset and/or expense accounts in vintrace](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301385387668) and [linked to Xero from the client billing item](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303326190356), or from the stock category.

Syncing a purchase order raises an accounts payable invoice in Xero where you can manage its payment. Vendor information is also sent to Xero when the invoice is created eliminating the need for the vendor to pre-exist in Xero.

Purchase orders with a *New* status will be created in Xero as a *Draft* invoice. To ensure the invoice is created as an approved (i.e., awaiting payment) invoice in Xero, be sure that its status in vintrace is *Approved*.

To sync purchase orders with Xero, [create a purchase order](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303315399444) in vintrace and be sure to select the Sync to Xero checkbox.

## Viewing the Purchase Order in Xero

To view the purchase order in Xero, click the View in Accounts link that’s on the purchase order.
