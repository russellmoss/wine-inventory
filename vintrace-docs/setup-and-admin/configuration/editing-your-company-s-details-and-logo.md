# Editing Your Company's Details and Logo

Your company’s name and logo will display on your reports, bills of lading, and other documents. If you need to update your company's name or logo, please contact our support team.

The logo file needs to be in JPG format; if it is not convert it to jpg if possible. The logo is suggested to be at least 150×300; any smaller and it may suffer from pixelization (jagged, blurred image).
