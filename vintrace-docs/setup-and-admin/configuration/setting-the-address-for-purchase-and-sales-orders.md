# Setting the Address for Purchase and Sales Orders

In order to include your company’s name and address on purchase orders and sales orders, you’ll need to [update the company’s billing address](#Updating_Company_Billing_Address), then [update the winery’s billing information](#Updating_Winery_Billing_Info).

![Billing_Address_on_Purchase_Order_and_Sales_Order_20201111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329116130964)

## Updating the Company’s Billing Address

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329161271572) More Options in the sidebar.
2. From the Address Book tile, click Open Address Book.
3. Select your company.
4. Click Advanced.

![Basic_Organization_Widget_-_Advanced_Button_20201111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329161259284)

5. Select the Addresses tab.
6. In the Billing Address section, enter your company’s billing address.

![Organization_-_Addresses_-_Billing_Address_20201111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329129319956)

7. Click Save.

## Updating the Winery’s Billing Information

1. Click ![Setup_Icon_20200318.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329161283348) Set Up in the sidebar.
2. Click Locations.
3. From the Winery tile, click Configure.
4. Select the winery.
5. Select the Client Billing tab.
6. Select a billing company.

![Winery_Update_-_Client_Billing_-_Billing_Company_20201111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329161297684)

7. Click Save.
