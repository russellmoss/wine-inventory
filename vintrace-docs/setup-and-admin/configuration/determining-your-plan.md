# Determining Your Plan

The availability of certain vintrace features depends on your [winery's plan](https://www.vintrace.com/tus/pricing-and-plans/). To determine the type of plan that you have:

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328902053780) More Options in the sidebar.
2. From the Tools tile, click About My License. The Current Licence Details window displays your winery's licence type.

![Current_Licence_Details_-_Licence_Type_20210309.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328923834260)
