# Printing Recommendations for QR Codes

## Recommended Printers

The below printers are recommend to print QR codes in vintrace. You can purchase these directly from the links below.

Australia/New Zealand:

- DYMO LabelWriter 550
- Dymo LabelWriter 550 Turbo

USA:

- DYMO LabelWriter 550
- DYMO LabelWriter 550 Turbo
- Zebra GK420T (or similar)

**Please note: Only official DYMO labels that were manufactured after April 2020 will print in the 550 series of printers.** **Any 3rd party/generic labels will not print in these printers.**

[Other printers](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303330395284) that support printing a single label at a time will also be compatible.

## Recommended Labels

The following labels are recommended as they are polypropylene-based, which are water-resistant and more durable than standard paper labels. If printing from other labels you will likely need to select "Fit to Page" in the Scaling options of your Print dialog.

### DYMO

Dimensions are 59x101mm (2-5/16" x 4")

Australia:

- [DYMO Durable Large Shipping Labels](https://www.amazon.com.au/gp/product/B01LYV6HQW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=247&creative=1211&creativeASIN=B01LYV6HQW&linkCode=as2&tag=vintrace-22&linkId=ab6902e4761d3525d6fb4b9d9e333db7)

USA

- [DYMO Durable Large Shipping Labels](https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B01M1R5YOW/ref=as_li_tl?ie=UTF8&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=B01M1R5YOW&linkCode=as2&tag=vintraceusa-20&linkId=6394829b22f8c6fc2c214795a57436e6)

### Zebra

- Zebra PolyPro 3000T (18927); dimensions are 76mm x 76mm (3" x 3")

The Zebra PolyPro 3000T label does not fit DYMO printers.

- Zebra PolyPro 4000T (10011689); dimensions are 51mm x 76mm (3" x 2")
