# Customizing the Vineyards & Blocks Page

You can customize the [Vineyards & Blocks page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301313410196) by [changing the columns that are displayed](#h_01GF1RF55PEA0N5FNPYP69BS74), and their [display order](#h_01GF1RFD9136P4NPD53ZJWFJVP).

## Changing the Columns Displayed

To change the details displayed on the Vineyards & Blocks page:

1. Click the ![Gear_Green_Icon_20200417.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329206977428) gear icon that's located to the right of the column headers.
2. Select the details you want displayed on the page.
3. Click X to close the Select Columns window.

![Customizing_Columns_20221010.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329198416276)

## Changing the Display Order of Columns

To change the order of the columns displayed on the Vineyards & Blocks page, click the column header and drag it to its new position.
