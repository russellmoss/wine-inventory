# Recording a Block's Disease Status

To record a block’s disease status, [record an assessment](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000826036-Recording-Seasonal-Block-and-Viticulture-Assessments#RecordingaNewAssessment) and select the disease status from the Disease Status list.

![Create Block Assessment - Disease Status 20240207.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329205727124)

The selected disease status displays in the block’s Seasonal tab.

![Block Overview - Seasonal Tab - Disease Status 20240207.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329210743956)

You will not be able to [book or receive fruit](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000814175) if the block’s most recent disease status is Disease Assessment or Rejected.
