# About Fruit Sampling in vintrace

In vintrace, you'll need to [create a sample set](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301351265172) for every block that you record fruit samples for. Sample sets act as a grouping mechanism and allow you to record individual samples for an extended period of time. We recommend configuring all your sample sets well ahead of vintage in order to save time when it comes to [recording analysis readings](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301319766292).

The [Sample Set Console](#Accessing_Sample_Set_Console) lets you [create sample sets](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301351265172), [record analyses](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301319766292) and [tasting notes](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301351165204), [correct measurements](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301305213716), and [view results](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301351220372).

## Accessing the Sample Set Console

To access the Sample Set Console:

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328975110932) More Options from the sidebar.
2. From the Harvest tile, click Maturity Samples.
