# Removing a Fruit Booking and Fruit Intake

## Making a Booking Inactive

If you haven’t received any fruit against a booking, you can mark the booking as inactive:

1. Click ![Fruit_Intake_Icon_20200403.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329050536724) Fruit Console in the sidebar.
2. Search for the booking.
3. Click the down arrow beside the Arrive button.
4. Select Booking > Edit.

![Arrive_Button_-_Booking_-_Edit_20200407.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329045028628)

The Update Scale Booking window displays.

5. Check the Inactive checkbox.
6. Click Save.

## Removing a Fruit Intake

If you’ve received fruit against a booking in error, you can remove it from the Fruit Intake console in its entirety by doing the following:

1. Click ![Fruit_Intake_Icon_20200403.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329050536724) Fruit Console in the sidebar.
2. Search for the booking.
3. Click the down arrow beside the Arrive button.
4. Select Completed Intakes > Reverse.

![Arrive_Button_-_Completed_Intakes_-_Reverse_20200407.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329060418708)

A confirmation message displays.

5. Click OK.

Refer to our [Correcting a Fruit Intake article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303331960980) to learn more.

If you’ve already performed a crush/press operation, you’ll need to perform a rollback. Refer to our [rollback and replay article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303277767188) to learn more.
