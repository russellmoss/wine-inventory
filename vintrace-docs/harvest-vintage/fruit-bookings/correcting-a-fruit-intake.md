# Correcting a Fruit Intake

It’s easy to correct most things on a fruit intake such as the wrong weight, wrong fruit source information, or even the truck/delivery details.

To correct a fruit intake:

1. Click ![Fruit_Intake_Icon_20200403.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329037597076) Fruit Intake in the sidebar.
2. Search for the booking.
3. Click the down arrow beside the Arrive button.
4. Select Completed Intakes > Correct.

![Arrive_Button_-_Completed_Intakes_-_Correct_20200408.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329058372500)

The Select a Delivery to Correct window displays.

5. Click the delivery you want to correct. The Intake Details window displays so that you can correct the details as needed.

Refer to our [Remove a Fruit Booking article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303317457812) to learn more.
