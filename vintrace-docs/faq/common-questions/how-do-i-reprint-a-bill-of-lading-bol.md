# How do I reprint a Bill of Lading (BOL)?

To reprint a bill of lading:

1. Click ![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328936271508) More Options from the sidebar.
2. From the Tools tile, click Dispatch Search.

![Tools_Tile_-_Dispatch_Search_20220323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328930079252)

The Dispatch Search window displays.

![Dispatch_Search_20220323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328960722708)

3. Use the filters to find your dispatch. If you know the dispatch number, you can enter it in the BOL# field.
4. Select the dispatch.
5. Click Bill of Lading.

![What_to_do_-_Bill_of_Lading_20220323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328930064020)

The Bill of Lading Declaration window displays.

![Bill_of_Lading_Declaration_20220323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328955721748)

6. Scroll down to specify the options that you want to include/exclude (e.g., composition, additives), or to select a BOL template.

![Bill_of_Lading_Declaration_Options_20220323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328960685588)

7. Click OK. A PDF of the bill of lading displays. You can email, save, or print the bill of lading.

![Bill_of_Lading_PDF_20220323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328936232980)

You can [assign a winery specific prefix](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301350503572) to your bills of lading.
