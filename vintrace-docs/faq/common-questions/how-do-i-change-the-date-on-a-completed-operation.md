# How do I change the date on a completed operation?

In order to change a completed operation's date, you must have the [Advanced Data Management permission](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000813755-Roles-and-Permissions#Permissions).

If you entered an incorrect date for a completed operation, you can correct the date.

To change the date of a completed operation:

1. Locate and view the operation. There are several ways to do this including from the Jobs tab of the [Product page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303310460948), or from the [Job Management Console](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303318317972).
2. Click Fix Date.

![Racking_-_Fix_Date_Button_20210312.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328994008340)

The Fix Process Date window displays.

![Fix_Process_Date_20210312.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329002854036)

3. Enter the new date for the operation. The date will be restricted to a certain range depending on when the previous operation was recorded.
4. Click OK.
