# How do I adjust a wine batch's volume?

You can use the Measurement operation to adjust the volume of a wine batch:

1. Do any of the following:

- From the [Vessels page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301344827924) or [Job Management page](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303318317972), click the ![Operations_Icon_20200407.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329214654100) operations icon, then select Measurement.

![Operations_Icon_-_Vessels_and_Job_Mgmt_Pages_20210217.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329252439828)

- From the [Product page,](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303310460948) click the ![Add_Measurement_Icon_20200901.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329231374996) measurement icon. This icon displays for tanks, bins, and wines that are in single barrels, kegs, and carboys.

![Measurement_Icon_20210217.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329225034900)

2. From the Measurement window's General tab, enter the volume in the New Amount field.
3. Click the ![Calculator_Icon_20200410.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329224928020)calculator icon beside the Loss field. vintrace calculates the loss or gain.

![Changing_Batch_Volume_20210208.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329252383892)

4. If you entered a volume that’s less than what was previously entered, select the Loss Reason.
5. Click Save.

For details on adjusting a barrel group’s volume, refer to our [Changing a Barrel Group’s Details article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360000813555/#ChangingaBarrel'sVolume).
