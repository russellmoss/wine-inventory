# How do I launch multiple windows in vintrace?

To open another vintrace window, click the plus icon located at the bottom of the sidebar.

![Opening_Another_vintrace_20210312.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329276805140)

This opens another vintrace tab in your browser without needing to log in again.
