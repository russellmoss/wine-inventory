# Troubleshooting Your TTB report

Because errors happen, your [TTB Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303292459668) may not add up perfectly. When this happens, refer to the following articles to identify and correct errors before re-running the TTB Report:

- [Tax Breakdown Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303292935188)
- [Tax Event Console](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303292976276)

If, after reading these articles, you’re still unable to resolve issues in your TTB Report, contact your Compliance Officer, the TTB, or [support@vintrace.com](mailto:support@vintrace.com).
