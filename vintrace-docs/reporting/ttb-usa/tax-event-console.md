# Tax Event Console

In addition to the [Tax Breakdown Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303292935188), the Tax Event Console provides a way to [troubleshoot your TTB Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303286690708). The Tax Event Console shows all TTB events for a bond and tax class during the specified date range.

![Tax_Event_Console_20210111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328980679956)

To access the Tax Event Console:

1. Click![More_Options_20200323.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328992737428) More Options in the sidebar.
2. From the TTB Tile, click Tax event console.
3. Specify the filters.
4. Click Search.

You can also access the Tax Event Console by clicking the Launch Tax History to Compare link from any of the government reports (e.g., [TTB Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303292459668)) in the Winery Reports window.
![Winery_Reports_-_Government_-_Launch_Tax_History_Link_20210111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328992781716)

The events that meet the filters are displayed into sections that correspond to the same sections in the [TTB Report](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303292459668).

![Tax_Event_Console_-_Components_20210111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328980662932)

You can click in the Operation column to view the operation that caused the event. To view the wine affected by the event, click the link in the Target column.

You can also [compare the events to the TTB Report](#Comparing_Events). Users with the [Can Edit Tax Volume Events permission](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303349421588) can [edit the tax volume event's details](#Editing_Tax_Volume_Event_Details).

## Comparing Events to TTB Report

To compare the TTB events with the TTB report:

1. Click Compare Against TTB Reports located in the lower left of the window.
2. Click the preferred option.

![Tax_Event_Console_-_Compare_Against_Tax_Report_Options_20210111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329001347988)

## Editing Tax Volume Event Details

You must have the [Can Edit Tax Volume Events permission](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303349421588) to edit the tax volume event’s details.

Any changes that you make to the tax volume event will impact the TTB Report. It will not change the operation or batch/stock item that created the TTB tax event.

To edit the tax volume event’s details:

1. Click the ![Pencil_Gray_Icon_20200414.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329009509780) pencil icon beside the tax event.

![Tax_Event_Console_-_Editing_20210111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329009541396)

The Tax Volume Event window displays.

![Tax_Volume_Event_Update_20210111.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328992853780)

2. Edit the details as needed.
3. Click Save.
