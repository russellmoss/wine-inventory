# Adding Notes and Attachments to Work Orders on the Mobile App

You can add a note or attachment to any step of a job.

To add notes and attachments to a work order:

1. From the main screen, tap Work Orders.

![Attachments_1.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329068519188)

2. Tap the work order that you want to add a note or attachment to.

![Attachments_2.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329060432916)

3. Tap the ![Paperclip_20201020.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329027825300) paperclip icon.

![Attachments_3.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329060473108)

4. Enter your note in the field.
5. To attach an image, tap the ![Camera_Plus_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329040216596) camera icon, select the type of attachment, then take a photo.

![Attachments_4.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329027671444)

6. Tap the ![Checkmark_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329060673812) checkmark icon to save your note and attachment.

![Attachments_8.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329050722836)
