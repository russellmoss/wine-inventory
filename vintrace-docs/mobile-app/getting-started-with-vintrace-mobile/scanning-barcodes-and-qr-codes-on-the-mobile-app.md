# Scanning Barcodes and QR Codes on the Mobile App

You can use the mobile app to scan barcodes and QR codes for [vessels](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303321067412), [lab requests](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301385573012), [stock items](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301305894932), and [storage areas](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301320534420).

To scan a barcode or QR code from the mobile app:

1. Tap the barcode icon from the main screen.

![Barcode_Scanner_20201005.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329275909908)

2. Scan the barcode or QR code on the vessel, lab request, stock item, or storage area.

Scanning storage areas and bay QR codes are only for the [Adjustment and Move operations](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301336227220). Refer to our [Scanning Lab Barcodes to View and Record Lab Results article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301385573012) for details on using the mobile app to view and record lab results.
