# Resetting Your Password on the Mobile App

To reset your password from the mobile app:

1. Tap Forgot Your Password at the login screen.

![Reset_password.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328635615892)

2. Specify the information requested.

![Reset_password_2.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328621285012)

3. Tap Submit.
