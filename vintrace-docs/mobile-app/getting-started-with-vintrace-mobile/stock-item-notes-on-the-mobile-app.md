# Stock Item Notes on the Mobile App

## Adding a Note to a Stock Item

To add notes and attachments (e.g., photos) to a stock item:

1. Search for and view the stock item.
2. Tap the ![Observation Tab Icon 20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329220964628) icon.
3. Tap Add Notes and Attachments.
4. Enter your notes.
5. To attach photos:

- Tap the ![Camera Plus 20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329220976020) camera icon.
- Tap the type of photo you want to attach.
- To attach a new photo, tap Camera. To attach an existing photo, tap Photos.

6. Tap the checkmark to save your notes and photos.

## Deleting a Note from a Stock Item

To delete a note from a stock item:

1. Search for and view the stock item.
2. Tap the ![Observation Tab Icon 20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329220964628) icon.
3. Tap the note you'd like to delete.
4. Tap the ![Trash 20240328.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329226023060) icon.
