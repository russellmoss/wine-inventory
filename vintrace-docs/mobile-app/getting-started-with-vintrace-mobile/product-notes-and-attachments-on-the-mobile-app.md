# Product Notes and Attachments on the Mobile App

## Adding a Note to Wine

To add notes and attachments to your wine from the mobile app:

1. Search for and view the wine.
2. Tap the ![Note_Tap_Icon_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328569428116) Notes tab.

![2021PNPR-BW_-_Notes_Tab_20220525.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328588353812)

3. Tap Add Note.
4. From the Link To menu, select the wine.

![2021PNPR-BW_-_Notes_Tab_-_Link_To_Wine_20220525.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328560658708)

5. Enter your note in the field.

![2021PNPR-BW_-_Note_for_Wine_20220525.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328588376596)

6. To attach an image:

- Tap the ![Camera_Plus_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328569416980) camera icon.
- Tap the type of image you want to attach.

![202PNPR-BW_-_Note_for_Wine_-_Image_Type_20220525.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328560726804)

- Use your device’s camera to take the photo.
- When you’re ready to upload the photo, tap Use Photo.

7. Tap the ![Checkmark_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328575235092) checkmark to save your note and attachment, or X to discard it.

You can also add notes and attachments to your wine from the vintrace web application. Refer to our [Adding Notes and Attachments to Wine article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303291025940) for details.

## Deleting a Note

To delete a note from a wine:

1. Search for and view the wine.
2. Tap the ![Note_Tap_Icon_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328569428116) Notes tab.
3. Tap the note you'd like to delete.
4. Tap the ![Trash 20240328.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328575205140) icon.
