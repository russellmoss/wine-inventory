# Viewing and Recording Fruit Samples for a Block on the Mobile App

You can view and record a block’s fruit samples from the ![Lab_Results_Tab_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329235662100) Lab Results tab.

## Recording a Fruit Sample

To record a fruit samples for a [block](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303310811412):

1. Do one of the following:

- From the ![Lab_Results_Tab_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329235662100) Lab Results tab, tap Add Result.

![Lab_Results_-_Add_Result_Button_20200914.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329218997268)

- Tap the + in the upper right, then tap Record Lab.

![Actions_-_Record_Lab_20200914.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329189923476)

2. Tap the metric that you want to add.

![Labs_-_Add_Results_List_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329189941012)

3. Enter the metric's value.
4. Tap OK to save the metric's value, or tap X to discard your changes.

## Editing a Fruit Sample

To edit a fruit sample:

1. From the ![Lab_Results_Tab_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329235662100) Lab Results tab, tap the sample you want to edit.
2. Tap Edit.
3. Enter the metric's value.
4. Tap OK to save the metric's value, or tap X to discard your changes.
