# Tracking Ferments, Pump Overs and Punch Downs on the Mobile App

## Starting a Ferment

To start a ferment from the mobile app:

1. Search for and view the wine.
2. Tap the ![Add_Icon_20201027.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329194143508) add icon.

![Start_Ferment_1.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329188655380)

3. Tap Ferment Activity.

![Start_Ferment_2.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329193962900)

4. To enter the results for brix and temperature, tap Enter Brix (or Baume) and Enter Temp.

![Start_Ferment_3.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329234348052)

The last result displays the next time you record a ferment activity.

5. Tap Submit.

![Start_Ferment_4.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329194062356)

## Cap Management

You can do any of the following from the mobile app:

- Record a pump over
- Record a punch down
- View the extracted juice or fermenting wine

To perform a pump over or punch down:

1. Search for and view the wine.
2. Tap the ![Add_Icon_20201027.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329194143508) add icon.
3. Tap Ferment Activity.
4. If lab analysis isn’t required, turn off the Lab Analysis toggle.

![Cap_Management_1.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329174167060)

5. Tap the Cap Management toggle to enable it.
6. Tap Activity.
7. Select Pump Over or Punch Down, then tap the ![Checkmark_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329217902612) checkmark icon.
8. If you want to enter a duration, tap Duration, select the duration, then tap the ![Checkmark_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329217902612) checkmark icon.
9. Tap Submit.

You can [attach notes and/or photos](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301354570132) by tapping the ![Paperclip_20201020.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329217961748) paperclip icon in the upper right.

## Stopping a Ferment

To stop a ferment from the mobile app:

1. Search for and view the wine.
2. Tap the tile that shows the last ferment activity.

![Stop_Ferment_1.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329188699668)

3. Tap Has Fermentation Stopped to select the checkbox.

![Stop_Ferment_2.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329174104212)

4. Tap Submit.
