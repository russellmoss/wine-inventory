# Viewing Blocks on the Mobile App

You can search for blocks in the vintrace app by scanning their QR code or typing their name in the search field from the app home screen. You can also search for a variety from the search field, which will display all blocks featuring that grape variety.

Blocks returned in the vintrace app's search results display a ![Block_White_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329203233428) or ![Block_Red_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329235223060) beside them (depending on the type of fruit).

![Blocks_in_Search_Results_20200914.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329203218964)

Scanning a QR code for a block will open the block instantly. When using the search function, you can view a block by tapping it's name in the search results.

The block’s name, variety, region/sub-AVA, vineyard, and grower display at the top of the screen. Below this information are tabs that display the block’s summary, [lab results](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301373156756), [observations](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301339832468), [bookings](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301356626324), [parcels/fruit intakes](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301356626324), and details.

![Block_Tabs_20200914.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329203249684)

On the ![Summary_Tab_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329203274132) Summary tab, the block’s harvest date, expected amount, received amount for each year displays. Below that is other information are lab results for the block. You may have to scroll horizontally to view lab results that aren’t visible on your screen.

Refer to our [Adding a Block article](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32303262642196) for details on adding a block from the mobile app.
