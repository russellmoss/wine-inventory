# Weighing Fruit in Bins on the Mobile App

To weigh fruit that you’ve received in bins from the mobile app:

1. Search for and view the block.
2. Tap the ![Add_Icon_20201027.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329190574868) add icon.
3. Tap Receive.

![Actions_-_Receive_20201027.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329236401812)

4. Tap Bin Scale.

![Receive_Fruit_-_Bin_Scale_20201027.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329204203796)

The selected option becomes your default for receiving fruit. To receive fruit using a truck scale, tap Bin Scale, then select Truck Scale.

5. Tap the Bins field.

![Receive_Fruit_-_Bin_Scale_-_Bins_20201027.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329204253460)

6. Do one of the following:

- Search for and select an existing bin, then enter the number and gross. The net will be automatically calculated.

![Bin_-_Net_Auto_Calculated_20201027.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329219491220)

- To create a new bin, enter the bin’s name, then tap Create.

![Bin_-_Creating_20201027.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329209207316)

Enter the tare, quantity, and gross for the new bin, then tap Update.

7. To finish the fruit intake, tap Review. Otherwise, tap Finish Later.
8. To finish the fruit intake, review the Receive Fruit Summary screen, then tap Submit.
