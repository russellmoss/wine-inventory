# Signing a Weigh Tag on the Mobile App

When you [receive fruit](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301356626324), you’ll have the option to provide a digital signature.

## Signing the Weigh Tag

When you receive fruit, you can provide your digital signature on the weigh tag on the Sign & Send screen.

To sign the weigh tag:

1. Tap Sign Here.

![Sign_and_Send_-_Sign_Here_20200909.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329184885524)

2. In the space provided, sign the weigh tag using your finger or a stylus.

![Sign_and_Send_3.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329156984084)

3. When you’re done signing, tap Done. If you need to start over, tap Reset.

vintrace remembers your signature for the next time you send a weigh tag.

## Editing Your Signature

After the first time you sign a weigh tag, vintrace remembers your signature. When you [receive fruit](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301356626324), your signature will be automatically added.

If you need to edit your signature, tap the ![Pencil_Icon_20200909.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329170757396) pencil beside your signature.
