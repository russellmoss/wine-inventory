# Barrel Notes and Attachments on the Mobile App

## Adding Notes and Attachments to a Specific Barrel

To add notes and attachments to a specific barrel from the mobile app:

1. Search for and view the wine.
2. Tap the ![Note_Tap_Icon_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328552638228) Notes tab.

![2021PNPR-BW_-_Notes_Tab_20220525.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328572791444)

3. Tap Add Note.
4. From the Link To menu, select *Specific Barrel*.

![2021PNPR-BW_-_Notes_Tab_-_Link_To_Specific_Barrel_20220525.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328567009044)

5. Search for then tap the barrel.

![202PNPR-BW_-_Note_-_Barrel_Search_20220525.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328572690708)

6. Enter your note in the field.

![2021PNPR-BW_-_Note_for_Barrel_20220525.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328583096084)

7. To attach an image:

- Tap the ![Camera_Plus_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328567017876) camera icon.
- Tap the type of image you want to attach.
- To attach a new photo, tap Camera. To attach an existing photo, tap Photos.

8. Tap the ![Checkmark_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328542017812) checkmark to save your note and attachment, or X to discard it.

## Deleting a Note

To delete a note:

1. View the note.
2. Tap the ![Trash 20240328.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328542079764) icon.

![Delete Note 20240328.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328583194516)

You'll be asked to confirm that you want to delete the note.

3. Tap Delete to delete the note. Tap Cancel to retain the note.
