# Swapping Barrels/Kegs Within a Barrel Group on the Mobile App

You can swap barrels/kegs in the barrel group when you submit a transfer operation from the mobile app. The example below shows how to swap one of the barrels to be emptied with a keg.

1. From the Empty Barrels step of the Transfer operation, tap the ![List_Icon_20201026.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329187306132) list icon.

   [![Swap 1](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-1.jpg)](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-1.jpg)
2. Do either of the following to select the barrels to be emptied:

- Select the checkbox beside the barrels.

[![Swap 2](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-2.jpg)](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-2.jpg)

- Tap the  barcode icon and scan the barrels.

3. Tap the ![Checkmark_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329187324052) checkmark icon.

[![Swap 3](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-3.jpg)](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-3.jpg)

4. In the Swap In Barrels section of the screen, do either of the following:

- Tap the ![Keyboard_Green_BG_20201026.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329192743316) keyboard icon and enter the keg or barrel's name.

- Tap the ![Barcode_Green_BG_20201026.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329182693140) barcode icon and scan the keg or barrel.

[![Swap 4](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-4.jpg)](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-4.jpg)

5. In the Swap In Barrels screen:

1. Enter the name of the keg or barrel.
2. Select the fill type.
3. Tap the ![Checkmark_20200916.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329187324052) checkmark icon.

[![Swap 5](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-5.jpg)](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-5.jpg)

6. Tap Submit.

[![Swap 6](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-6.jpg)](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/vintrace-support-site-content/uploads/2017/11/Swap-6.jpg)
