# Moving Barrels on the Mobile App

You can manage the location of your barrels from the mobile app by scanning them, then selecting their new location.

1. View the details of the barrels you want to move, then tap the + icon.
   ![Movebarrels1a.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329123361556)
2. From the Actions menu, tap Move Barrels.
   ![Actions_-_Move_Barrels_20200521.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329123310996)
3. Scan or select the barrels you want to move, then tap Next. If you're moving barrels in a barrel group, all barrels in the group will be moved.
   ![Movebarrels3.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329089216916)
4. Select the building and storage area(s) you want to move the barrels to, then tap Review.
   ![Movebarrels4.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329097921300)
5. Review the operation, then tap Submit.
   ![Movebarrels5.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329136634644)

The barrels are moved to the selected location.

![Movebarrels6a.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329097830420)
