# Dip Calculator on the Mobile App

The dip calculator is only available for [tanks with a dip chart](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/360001936716).

To access a dip calculator from vintrace mobile:

1. View the details of the tank, then tap the + icon.

![Tank - Plus Icon 20240424.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329238188564)

2. From the Actions menu, tap Dip Calculator.

![Dip Calculator 20240424.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329238211732)

The dip calculator displays.

![IMG_7270.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329196975636)

You can do a calculation from the dip or volume.

![IMG_7271.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329196959124)

Or calculate the volume between two dips.

![IMG_7272.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32329205542804)
