# Editing, Reversing, and Backdating Labs on the Mobile App

You can [edit](#h_01ENKCE96M5RS9VTZQP92NZ36Z), [reverse](#h_01ENKCEFVP6S1BTCJ1K1DA03QD), and [backdate](#h_01ENKCEN8XFHHEZ87BP1TWP8Z2) labs from the mobile app.

## Editing Lab Results

To edit a lab result from the mobile app:

1. Tap the ![Lab_Results_Tab_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328903669780) Lab Results tab.
2. Tap the ![Pencil_Gray_20201026.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328903651220)pencil icon.

![Edit_1.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328880665620)

3. Tap Edit Result.

![Edit_2.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328948728084)

4. Enter the new value, then tap OK.

![Edit_3.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328948765076)

## Reversing Lab Results

To reverse a lab result from the mobile app:

1. Tap the ![Lab_Results_Tab_Icon_20200914.PNG](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328903669780) Lab Results tab.
2. Tap the ![Pencil_Gray_20201026.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328903651220) pencil icon.

![Edit_1.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328880665620)

3. Tap Reverse.

![Reverse_1.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328912069652)

## Backdating Lab Results

To backdate a lab result from the mobile app:

1. [Add a lab result](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/en-us/articles/32301385573012). By default, this sets the analysis to the current date and time.
2. Tap ![Clock_Icon_20201026.png](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328948943892) Just Now.

![Backdate_1.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328903553172)

3. Select the analysis’ date and time, then tap OK.

![Backdate_3.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328921050132)

The selected date and time displays on the analysis.

4. Tap OK.

This sets the analysis to a date in the past.

![Backdate_5.jpg](https://support.vintrace.com/hc/article_attachments/32328880768788)
