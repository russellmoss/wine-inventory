Editorial brand patterns lifted straight from the deck.

```jsx
<Eyebrow rule>What it includes</Eyebrow>
<Metric value="$2.4B" caption="Assets under advisement" />
<Quote name="Maya Iyer" role="Founder, Iyer Wealth">
  Savvy gave our advisors their time back.
</Quote>
```

- **Eyebrow** — uppercase tracked kicker; tones `gold` / `ink` / `onDark`, optional `rule`.
- **Metric** — large Inter Tight Light figure + caption; `serif` swaps to Big Caslon; sizes `sm`/`md`/`lg`.
- **Quote** — oversized serif quote mark + Big Caslon text + attribution. Set `onDark` for the black register; override `markSrc` if the bundled relative asset path doesn't resolve from your page.
